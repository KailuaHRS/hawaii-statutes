[§183D-13] Interstate wildlife violator compact. (a) The department may enter into the interstate wildlife violator compact or similar agreement, whether in the form of a memorandum of understanding, written agreement, or mutual aid compact, with an appropriate state, federal, or foreign entity for mutual assistance in the enforcement of hunting, fishing, and other wildlife laws, rules, or regulations in their respective jurisdictions. (b) The department may adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 necessary to implement this section. [L 2021, c 142, §2] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
