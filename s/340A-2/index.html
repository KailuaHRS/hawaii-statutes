§340A-2 Ownership of solid waste. Unless otherwise provided by county ordinance or federal regulation, the owner of solid waste shall be: (1) The generator of the solid waste until the solid waste is collected; (2) The collector until the solid waste is deposited with an operator; (3) In the absence of a collector, the generator of the solid waste until the solid waste is deposited with an operator; or (4) The operator upon acceptance of the solid waste.
