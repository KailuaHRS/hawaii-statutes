§329-12

Nomenclature.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§329-12] Nomenclature. The controlled substances listed or to be listed in the schedules in sections 329-14, 329-16, 329-18, 329-20, and 329-22 are included by whatever official, common, usual, chemical, or trade name designated. [L 1972, c 10, pt of §1] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
