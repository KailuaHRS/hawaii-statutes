Record of acknowledgments to be kept.

⚖ Official version ↗
PART VII. RECORDS OF ACKNOWLEDGMENTS Note Sections 502-71 to 502-74 designated as Part VII and part heading amended by L 2009, c 102, §2(8). §502-71 Record of acknowledgments to be kept. All judges and other officers authorized by law to take acknowledgments to instruments, besides the certificate of acknowledgment indorsed upon the instrument, shall keep a record of every acknowledgment in a book of records. Each record shall set forth at least the date of acknowledgment, the parties to the instrument, the persons acknowledging, the date, and some memorandum as to the nature of the instrument acknowledged.
