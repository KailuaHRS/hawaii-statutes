§37-52 Provisions applicable to special funds. All special funds maintained by all departments, commissions, boards, agencies, and establishments of the State shall be governed by this chapter, provided that departments and establishments shall not be authorized to make expenditures out of any special fund in excess of the moneys available in the special fund.
