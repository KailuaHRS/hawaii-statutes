[§672B-8] Design claim conciliation panel hearing; persons attending. Unless excluded or excused by the panel, the following persons shall attend hearings before the panel: (1) The party or parties making the claim; (2) The design professional against whom the claim is made or representatives thereof, other than counsel, authorized to act for the design professional; or (3) Counsel for the parties, if any. [L 2007, c 207, pt of §2] Revision Note In paragraph (2), the semicolon after "thereof" changed to a comma pursuant to §23G-15. Previous Vol13_Ch0601-0676 Next
