[§672B-8] Design claim conciliation panel hearing; persons attending. Unless excluded or excused by the panel, the following persons shall attend hearings before the panel: (1) The party or parties making the claim; (2) The design professional against whom the claim is made or representatives thereof, other than counsel, authorized to act for the design professional; or (3) Counsel for the parties, if any.
