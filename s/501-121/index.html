Leases; registration required.

⚖ Official version ↗
LEASES §501-121 Leases; registration required. Leases of registered land for a term of one year or more shall be registered. [L 1903, c 56, §64; RL 1925, §3253; RL 1935, §5063; RL 1945, §12663; am L 1951, c 142, §4; RL 1955, §342-63; HRS §501-121] Cross References Land subject to mortgage or lease, see §501-29. Case Notes Purchaser of lease noted on certificate of title holds free of unregistered sublease. 33 H. 343 (1935). Cited: 60 H. 40, 587 P.2d 294 (1978). Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
