[§477E-6] Inconsistency with federal laws. No creditor shall be required to comply with any provisions of this chapter that are directly inconsistent with federal laws relating to discrimination in the advancement of credit. A provision of this chapter is not directly inconsistent with federal laws if it provides greater protection to an applicant. [L 1975, c 109, pt of §1] Previous Vol11_Ch0476-0490 Next
