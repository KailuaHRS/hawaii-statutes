§87A-16

Health benefits plan; carriers.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§87A-16] Health benefits plan; carriers. (a) The board shall establish the health benefits plan or plans, which shall be exempt from the minimum group requirements of chapter 431. (b) The board may contract for health benefits plans or provide health benefits through a noninsured schedule of benefits. [L 2001, c 88, pt of §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
