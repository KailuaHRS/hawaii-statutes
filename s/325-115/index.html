⚖ Official version ↗
[§325-115] Program oversight committee. The director shall appoint a sterile needle exchange program oversight committee to provide assistance and advice in the oversight of the program. The committee shall meet periodically with the director to monitor the progress and effectiveness of the program and to examine available data compiled by the program.
