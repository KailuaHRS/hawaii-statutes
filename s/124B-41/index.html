PART V. COMPOSITION OF COURTS-MARTIAL [§124B-41] Who may convene general courts-martial. (a) General courts-martial may be convened by: (1) The governor; (2) The adjutant general; (3) The commanding officer of a force of the state military forces; (4) The commanding officer of a division or separate brigade; or (5) The commanding officer of a separate wing. (b) If any commanding officer authorized under subsection (a) is an accuser, the court shall be convened by superior competent authority and may in any case be convened by the superior authority if considered desirable by the authority.
