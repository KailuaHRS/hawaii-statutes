§328-17.5 Principal labeler responsibility under recall of drug. Whenever the manufacturer of a drug voluntarily recalls the drug or the Federal Food and Drug Administration or a court orders the recall of a drug, the principal labeler of the drug shall remove the drug from all pharmacies, prescriber offices, medical oxygen distributors, distributors of non-prescription drugs, and health care facilities.
