§572B-2 Eligibility to enter into a civil union. A person shall be eligible to enter into a civil union only if the person is: (1) Not a partner in another civil union or a spouse in a marriage; (2) At least eighteen years of age; and (3) Not related to the other proposed partner in the civil union, as provided in section 572B‑3.
