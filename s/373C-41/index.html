PART V. HAWAII OCCUPATIONAL EMPLOYMENT PLANNING SYSTEM §373C-41 Powers and duties. The Hawaii occupational employment planning system shall be part of the Hawaii occupational information system. It shall develop and deliver occupational employment demand and supply information used for planning purposes by employment, training, vocational education, vocational rehabilitation, economic development, and other agencies as well as private industry and individuals. The system shall be based on standardized and nonduplicative techniques and processes to the maximum extent practicable. [L 1983, c 193, pt of §1] Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
