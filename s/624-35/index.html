§624-35

Costs.

⚖ Official version ↗
PART III. COSTS Note Part heading added by L 1972, c 143, §1(n). §624-35 Costs. The costs of depositions may be taxed in the bill of costs as provided by section 607-9. [L 1876, c 32, §9; RL 1925, §2575; RL 1935, §3869; RL 1945, §9855; RL 1955, §223-5; HRS §624-35; am L 1972, c 143, §1(g)] Case Notes No abuse of discretion found in trial judge taxing cost against losing party. 9 H. 563 (1894). Commissioner's fee is item of cost. 12 H. 83 (1899). Attorney for losing party responsible for costs of commission. 19 H. 319 (1909). Fees of foreign attorney taking deposition. 49 H. 311, 417 P.2d 636 (1966). Costs recoverable where depositions necessarily obtained for use in the case. 5 H. App. 558, 705 P.2d 535 (1985). Cited: 31 H. 242, 249 (1930). Previous Vol13_Ch0601-0676 Next
