§441-29 Application for license; fees. Every applicant for a license under this chapter shall file an application with the director in such form and setting forth such information as may be prescribed or required by the director and shall furnish such additional information bearing upon the issuance of the license as the director requires. In the case of a copartnership or corporation any officer may sign the application on behalf of the applicant. The application shall be accompanied by an application fee.
