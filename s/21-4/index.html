§21-4 Adoption of rules. Each investigating committee shall adopt rules, not inconsistent with law or any applicable rules of the legislature, governing its procedures, including the conduct of hearings. [L 1969, c 211, pt of §1] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
