[§132-35] Preparation of statistical reports. Every two years, the state fire marshal shall prepare statistical reports on the history and condition of state fire defenses and an analysis of contributing factors of fire causes for the period of the report. The reports shall be posted on the office's website and publicly available upon request, subject to a fee not to exceed the cost of printing and distribution. The reports compiled under this section shall include a compilation or summary of the county fire chiefs' records prepared pursuant to section 132-1(a), in addition to any other sources the state fire marshal deems appropriate.
