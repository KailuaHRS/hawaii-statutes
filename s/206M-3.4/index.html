[§206M-3.4] Contracts for services necessary for management and operation of corporation. The corporation may contract with others, public or private persons, for the provision of all or a portion of the services necessary for the management and operation of the corporation. The corporation shall have the power to use all appropriations, grants, contractual reimbursements, and all other funds not appropriated for a designated purpose to pay for the proper general expenses and to carry out the purposes of the corporation.
