§147-95 Regulation of advertising. The department of agriculture and biosecurity may regulate advertising in regard to grading, inspection, and origin of carcasses or parts thereof by slaughterers, wholesalers, and retailers of beef, pork, or poultry carcasses offered for sale, regardless of the origin of such carcasses.
