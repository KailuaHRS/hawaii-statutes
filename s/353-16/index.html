§353-16 Transfer of committed felon to federal institution. The director may effect the transfer of a committed felon to any federal correctional institution for imprisonment, subsistence, care, and proper employment of such a felon. [L 1987, c 338, pt of §3; am L 1992, c 267, §2] Cross References Interstate compact for the supervision of adult offenders, see chapter 353B. Interstate corrections compact, see chapter 355D.
