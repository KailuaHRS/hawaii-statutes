[§124B-193] Wrongful interference with adverse administrative proceeding. Any person subject to this chapter who, having reason to believe that an adverse administrative proceeding is pending against any person subject to this chapter, wrongfully acts with the intent to: (1) Influence, impede, or obstruct the conduct of the proceeding; or (2) Otherwise obstruct the due administration of justice, shall be punished as directed by a court-martial.
