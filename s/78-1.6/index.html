§78-1.6

Office hours.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§78-1.6] Office hours. Offices of the State and counties shall be open for the transaction of public business as determined by the chief executive. Offices need not be open for the transaction of public business on the state holidays designated under section 8-1 and as observed under section 8-2.
