[§101-72] Negotiated purchase of private personal property under threat of condemnation. Any law requiring governmental purchase of personal property pursuant to competitive bidding to the contrary notwithstanding, private personal property, sought to be acquired under threat of the exercise of the power of eminent domain, may be acquired through negotiated purchase.
