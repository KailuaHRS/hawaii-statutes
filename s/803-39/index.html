[§803-39] Notice of warrantless search. Upon completion of any warrantless search of a house, store, or other building, the officer shall post notice of the search that shall include the report number, date, time, reason for entering, and office contact number for at least one of the officers involved in the search. Any entrances used by an officer shall be secured upon completion of a warrantless search. [L 2025, c 245, pt of §1] Previous Vol14_Ch0701-0853 Next
