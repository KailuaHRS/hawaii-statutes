§502-73 Same, open to inspection. The clerks of the circuit courts shall carefully preserve the books of record deposited with them as provided by section 502-72, filing the same with the records of the court. The records, both while in the custody of the acknowledging officers and after such filing, shall be open at all reasonable times to the inspection of any responsible person, without fee or reward.
