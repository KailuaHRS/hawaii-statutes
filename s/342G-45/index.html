[§342G-45] Establishment of an office paper and other materials recovery program. By January 1, 1992, the department shall initiate an office paper and other materials recovery program for all appropriate offices within the department. By June 30, 1993, all state and county agencies shall establish an office paper and other materials recovery program. [L 1991, c 324, pt of §2] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
