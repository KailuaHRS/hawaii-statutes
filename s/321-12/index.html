§321-12 Barbers, hairdressers, cosmeticians, cosmetologists, and beauticians. The department of health may prescribe rules and regulations which it deems necessary for the public health and safety relative to barbers, hairdressers, cosmeticians, cosmetologists, and beauticians, and the carrying on of their occupations. [L 1907, c 70, §1; RL 1925, §1020; RL 1935, §920; am L 1943, c 43, §5(a); RL 1945, §2016; am L 1945, c 140, §1; am L 1949, c 19, §2; RL 1955, §46-14; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §321-12] Cross References Barbering and cosmetology licensing act, see chapter 439A.
