§209-3 Duties of state and county department heads. Whenever the governor declares a state disaster all executive heads of departments and all county and city and county agencies shall: (1) Release personnel temporarily from their respective departments or agencies to work as needed under the direction of the rehabilitation coordinator; (2) Expedite the granting of rehabilitative assistance administered through their respective offices; (3) Provide the necessary information, records, and assistance for reporting on the rehabilitative efforts following a state disaster; and (4) Give full cooperation to the coordinator.
