§312-5 Annual report to the governor. Annually during the month of July but as of June 30 preceding, the state librarian shall report to the governor the moneys received from all sources and expended for all purposes during the preceding year, and any other matters pertaining to the libraries which it may deem important, or the governor may require. [L 1909, c 83, §5; RL 1925, §420; RL 1935, §803; am L 1937, c 33, §1; RL 1945, §1914; RL 1955, §45-4; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §18; HRS §312-5; am L 1981, c 150, §10; am L 2015, c 26, §7] Cross References Annual reports, see §93-12.
