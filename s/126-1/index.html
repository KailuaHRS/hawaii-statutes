§126-1 Authorization to director. The director of finance is authorized to enable the State to qualify and participate in the federal flood insurance program, pursuant to any applicable provisions of Public Law 1016, Eighty-fourth Congress, Second Session. The director of finance shall be vested with the functions, powers, and duties which are necessary to enable the State to qualify and participate in the flood insurance program.
