§9-22 State art museum; establishment. The state art museum is established within the foundation. The operations of the state art museum shall be funded and supported by: (1) Appropriations from the legislature; (2) Federal grants and subsidies; (3) Voluntary contributions; and (4) Funds received pursuant to section 9-3(7).
