§87A-31.1

Public employers; defined.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§87A-31.1] Public employers; defined. For the purposes of this part, "public employer" means a governmental entity whose employees', beneficiaries', and retirees' health benefits coverage is provided through the fund. [L 2013, c 268, pt of §6] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
