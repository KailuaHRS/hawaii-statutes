[§187A-1.6] Applicability of statutes and rules. This subtitle and any rules under this subtitle relating to fishing which are applicable to state marine waters shall also apply to the following persons in waters outside state marine waters but within the federal conservation zone: (1) Residents of the State; (2) Any commercial marine licensee; and (3) Any permittee or licensee under this subtitle.
