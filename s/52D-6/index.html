§52D-6

Police force; employees.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§52D-6] Police force; employees. The chief of police may appoint officers and other employees under such rules and at such salaries as are authorized by law. Probationary appointment, suspension, and dismissal of officers and employees of the police department shall be as authorized by law.
