[§240-1.5] Surcharge amounts exempt. Amounts received in the form of a monthly surcharge by a utility acting on behalf of an affected utility under section 269-16.3 shall not be gross receipts for the acting utility for purposes of this chapter. Any amounts retained by the acting utility for collection or other costs shall not be included in this exemption. [L 1993, c 337, §3] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
