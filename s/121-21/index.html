§121-21 Discharge. Discharges shall be granted for the convenience of the State and for the same reasons and in like manner as are from time to time prescribed by federal laws and regulations for the army or air national guard. [L 1967, c 196, pt of §1; HRS §121-21] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
