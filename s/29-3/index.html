§29-3 Appropriations. The governor may include in the governor's operating budget such sums as the governor deems necessary for the continuation and operations of the program. [L 1965, c 237, pt of §3; Supp, §12A-3; HRS §29-3; gen ch 1985] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
