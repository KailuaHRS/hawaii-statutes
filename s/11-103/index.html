[§11-103] Public notice of mailing. Public notice of the date or dates on which the initial ballot packages are to be mailed shall be given by the clerks before the ballot packages are made available to voters. [L 2019, c 136, pt of §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
