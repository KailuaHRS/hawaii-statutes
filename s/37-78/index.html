§37-78 Schedule of implementation. The governor shall submit to the legislature: (1) At the regular session of 1975, and every odd-numbered year's session thereafter, the program memoranda described in section 37-70. (2) At the regular session of 1975, and every odd-numbered year's session thereafter, the governor's proposed state budget and six-year program and financial plan.
