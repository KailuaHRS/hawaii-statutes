When set-aside required.

⚖ Official version ↗
§125C-22 When set-aside required. When a shortage as defined in section 125C-2 exists, all prime suppliers shall set aside supplies of each petroleum product for which there is a shortage. The amount set aside shall be in accordance with the rules adopted by the chief energy officer of the Hawaii state energy office.
