Sakada Day.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§8-18.5] Sakada Day. December 20 of each year shall be known and designated as "Sakada Day"; provided that this day is not and shall not be construed to be a state holiday. [L 2015, c 7, §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
