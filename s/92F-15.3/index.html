[§92F-15.3] Notice to the office of information practices. When filing a civil action that is under, related to, or is affected by this chapter, a person shall notify the office of information practices in writing at the time of the filing. The office of information practices may intervene in the action. [L 1998, c 82, §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
