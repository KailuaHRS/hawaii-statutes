§38-10 Interest on loan funds. All unexpended balances of state loan funds which have been allotted to the several counties for local improvements, and also all state loan funds which are so allotted, shall be deposited by the director in a depository in separate accounts. All interest received from any depository, on account of the separate deposits, shall be credited to the respective counties. [L 1970, c 51, pt of §1] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
