Partial enforcement.

⚖ Official version ↗
§576B-709 Partial enforcement. If a tribunal of this State does not recognize and enforce a Convention support order in its entirety, it shall enforce any severable part of the order. An application or direct request may seek recognition and partial enforcement of a Convention support order. [L 2015, c 77, pt of §1] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
