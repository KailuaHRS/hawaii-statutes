[§124B-170] Wearing unauthorized insignia, decoration, badge, ribbon, device, or lapel button. Any person subject to this chapter who: (1) Is not authorized to wear an insignia, decoration, badge, ribbon, device, or lapel button; and (2) Wrongfully wears any insignia, decoration, badge, ribbon, device, or lapel button upon the person's uniform or civilian clothing, shall be punished as directed by a court-martial.
