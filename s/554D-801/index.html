PART VIII. DUTIES AND POWERS OF TRUSTEE [§554D-801] Duty to administer trust. Upon acceptance of a trusteeship, the trustee shall administer the trust in good faith, in accordance with its terms and purposes and the interests of the beneficiaries, and in accordance with this chapter.
