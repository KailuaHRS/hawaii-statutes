[§269G-9] Report to legislature. No later than twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session, the commission shall submit a report to the legislature that includes: (1) An update on its activities under this chapter, including how the securitization authorized under this chapter is being utilized; and (2) Any other findings and recommendations, including any necessary proposed legislation.
