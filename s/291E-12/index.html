§291E-12 Persons qualified to take blood specimen. No person, other than a physician, physician assistant, registered nurse, or phlebotomist deemed qualified by the director of a clinical laboratory that is licensed by the State, or person licensed in a clinical laboratory occupation under section 321-13, may withdraw blood for the purpose of determining the alcohol concentration or drug content therein. This limitation shall not apply to the taking of a breath or urine specimen. [L 2000, c 189, pt of §23; am L 2009, c 151, §6] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
