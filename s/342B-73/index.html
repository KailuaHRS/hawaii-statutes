[§342B-73] Schedule of fees; establishment. The director may adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 that specify a schedule of fees to be paid by the sources of greenhouse gas emissions regulated pursuant to this part. The revenues collected pursuant to this section shall be deposited into the clean air special fund established under section 342B-32 to be used for the purposes thereof.
