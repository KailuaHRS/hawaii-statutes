[§801G-11] Prohibitions; penalty. (a) No applicant or program participant shall falsely attest that disclosure of the applicant's or program participant's actual address will endanger the applicant's or program participant's safety or knowingly provide false information on an initial application or an application for renewal. (b) An applicant or program participant who violates this section shall be fined not more than $500. [L 2018, c 115, pt of §1] Previous Vol14_Ch0701-0853 Next
