Names; registration of person required.]

⚖ Official version ↗
[§373L-1.5 Names; registration of person required.] No person within the purview of this chapter shall use the terms "professional employer organization", or "PEO", or other similar name unless the person is registered and in compliance with this chapter and the rules adopted pursuant to this chapter.
