[§448F-11] License without necessity of examination. All persons holding valid licenses as electrologists from the department of health and practicing on the day prior to June 25, 1990, shall be considered licensed under this chapter on June 25, 1990, without necessity of examination. [L 1990, c 285, pt of §1] Revision Note "June 25, 1990" substituted for "the effective date of this Act". Previous Vol10_Ch0436-0474 Next
