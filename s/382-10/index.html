§382-10 Powers in addition to other powers. The powers conferred by this chapter shall be in addition and supplemental to the powers conferred by any other law, including sections 125-1 to 125-7 and nothing herein shall be construed as limiting any other powers of the governor or the designated agency mentioned in section 382-3.
