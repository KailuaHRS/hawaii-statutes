Uniformity of application and construction.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§425-197] Uniformity of application and construction. This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among states enacting it. [L 2000, c 218, pt of §2] Previous Vol08_Ch0401-0429 Next
