§469-3 Renewal of license. Each licensed embalmer or licensed undertaker or funeral director shall, between July 1 and 10 of each year, renew the embalmer's or undertaker's or funeral director's license by registering with the department of health and by paying a renewal fee. Every license that is not so renewed shall expire on August 1 and shall not be renewed except upon the payment of a delinquent fee.
