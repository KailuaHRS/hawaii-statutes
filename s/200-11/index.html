[§200-11] Existing permits. An owner of a vessel used as a principal place of habitation holding a permit for that use in a state small boat harbor on June 9, 1976, may continue to moor the vessel in that harbor for such purpose and be permitted to obtain a new mooring permit; provided that the owner conforms to conditions set forth in sections 200-9 and 200-10.
