Animals, lien for care of.

⚖ Official version ↗
PART I. LIENS, GENERALLY Animals, Care of §507-1 Animals, lien for care of. Whoever pastures, feeds, or shelters animals by virtue of a contract with or by the consent of the owner of the animals for a compensation agreed upon, has a lien on the animals for pasturing, feeding, or sheltering to secure payment thereof with costs.
