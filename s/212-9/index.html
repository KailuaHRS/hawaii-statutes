§212-9 Special fund. There is established in the state treasury a fund to be known as the foreign-trade zones special fund. All fees or other moneys collected under this chapter shall be deposited in this fund. All moneys in the fund are hereby appropriated for the purposes of and shall be expended by the public corporation for the operation, capital improvement, and maintenance of the zone.
