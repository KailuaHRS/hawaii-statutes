§571-82 Court sessions; quarters. Sessions of the court shall be held at such places as the court shall determine, subject to section 603-14. [L 1965, c 232, pt of §1; Supp, §333-37; HRS §571-82; am L 1973, c 211, §1(j)] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
