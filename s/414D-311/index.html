[PART XVI.] SUPERSEDING CHAPTERS §414D-311 Superseding chapters. In the event of any conflict between the provisions of this chapter and the provisions of chapter 421J, 514B, or 514E, the provisions of chapter 421J, 514B, or 514E shall supersede and control the provisions of this chapter.
