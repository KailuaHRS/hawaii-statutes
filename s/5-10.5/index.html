State musical instrument; `auana; kahiko.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§5-10.5] State musical instrument; `auana; kahiko. (a) The `ukulele is established and designated as the official `auana musical instrument of the State. (b) The pahu is established and designated as the official kahiko musical instrument of the State. [L 2015, c 6, §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
