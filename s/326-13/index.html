§326-13 Expenses; rules. (a) The department of health shall bear all expenses of travel and other necessary expenses incurred under sections 326-1 to 326-14 and may adopt all rules and forms and perform all acts necessary and proper for carrying out their provisions. (b) Expenses related to patients shall be tracked separately from nonpatient costs, whenever appropriate and possible.
