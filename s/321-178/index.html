§321-178

Definitions.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§321-178] Definitions. As used in this part: "Client" means a child or adolescent who is receiving services from the child and adolescent mental health division. "Division" means the child and adolescent mental health division of the department of health. [L 2025, c 178, §2] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
