PART II. VOTING MACHINE SYSTEM Note Part heading amended by L 1975, c 36, §5(1). §16-11 Definitions. "Protective counter" means an apparatus built into the voting machine which cannot be reset, which records the total movement of the operating lever. "Voting machine system" means the method of electrically, mechanically, or electronically recording and counting votes upon being cast.
