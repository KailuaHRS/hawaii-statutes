[§432E-37] Binding nature of external review decision. (a) An external review decision shall be binding on the health carrier and the enrollee except to the extent that the health carrier or the enrollee has other remedies available under applicable federal or state law. (b) An enrollee or the enrollee's appointed representative shall not file a subsequent request for external review involving the same adverse action for which the enrollee has already received an external review decision pursuant to this part.
