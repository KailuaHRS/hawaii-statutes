§286-109.5 Designation of anatomical gift. The examiner of drivers shall design and implement a system to request anatomical gift information from all applicants for a driver's license or license renewal, at the time of application, including a method of directly imprinting on a license an applicant's designation of whether the applicant wishes to be an organ donor. The request shall elicit whether the applicant wishes to be an organ donor in the event of the applicant's death. [L 1975, c 80, §1; am L Sp 1977 1st, c 20, §12; gen ch 1985; am L 1990, c 18, §2] Note Developing an organ donor registry. L 2008, c 165.
