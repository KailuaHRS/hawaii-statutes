Reserve credit for reinsurance.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§432G-8] Reserve credit for reinsurance. Any dental insurer that takes credit for reserves on risks ceded to a reinsurer shall be subject to provisions of article 4A of chapter 431. [L 2013, c 191, pt of §1] Previous Vol09_Ch0431-0435H Next
