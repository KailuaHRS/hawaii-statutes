§6E-4 Administration. All state historic areas and buildings surplus to the operations of the department of accounting and general services shall be transferred by executive order to the department. All state projects and programs relating to historic preservation shall come under the authority of the department. [L 1976, c 104, pt of §2; am L 2015, c 147, §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
