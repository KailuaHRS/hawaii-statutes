§1-20 "Month", "year". The word "month" means a calendar month; and the word "year", a calendar year. [CC 1859, §18; RL 1925, §17; RL 1935, §18; RL 1945, §17; RL 1955, §1-25; HRS §1-20] Case Notes Cited: 10 H. 249, 250; 38 H. 443, 444. Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
