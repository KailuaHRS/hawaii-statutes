[§398A-2] Inapplicability. This chapter shall not apply to any leave of absence provided to any public employee serving as an organ or bone marrow donor pursuant to section 78-23.6. [L 2014, c 161, pt of §1] Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
