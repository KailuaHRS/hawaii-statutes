§321-198 State funding of substance abuse agencies. Certification of a private substance abuse agency pursuant to section 321-193(10), shall be a necessary prerequisite to that substance abuse agency receiving any state funding. This section shall take effect on December 31, 1979. [L 1977, c 108, §2; am L 1978, c 68, §1] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
