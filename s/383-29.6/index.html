[§383-29.6] Partial unemployment; eligibility. A new claim or an initial additional claim for partial unemployment benefits may be filed as the department prescribes for any week only if the individual: (1) Is a full-time worker; (2) Is attached to a regular employer, as defined in section 383-1; (3) Worked less than or did not work the individual's normal, customary full-time hours, as defined in section 383-1, for that week; (4) Had no earnings or earned less than the individual's weekly benefit amount for that week; and (5) Was unemployed due to a lack of full-time work, as defined in section 383-1, for that week.
