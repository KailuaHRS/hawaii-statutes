§46-64 Disposition of surplus property. Any other law to the contrary notwithstanding, any county may give, sell, or transfer any of its surplus personal property to the State or to any county within the State. [L 1965, c 251, §1; Supp, §138-54; HRS §46-64] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
