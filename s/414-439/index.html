§414-439 Resignation of registered agent of foreign corporation. The registered agent of a foreign corporation may resign from the registered agent's appointment by complying with the requirements of section 425R-10. [L 2000, c 244, pt of §1; am L 2001, c 129, §47; am L 2002, c 130, §32; am L 2009, c 55, §15] Previous Vol08_Ch0401-0429 Next
