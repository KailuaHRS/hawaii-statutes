Board of pharmacy; appointment; qualifications.

⚖ Official version ↗
§461-2 Board of pharmacy; appointment; qualifications. There shall be a board of pharmacy of seven members. Five members of the board shall be graduates of a school or college of pharmacy and shall have been licensed as pharmacists and actively engaged in the practice of pharmacy for at least five years prior to their appointment and two shall be public members. Four members of the board shall be residents of the city and county of Honolulu and three shall be residents of counties other than the city and county of Honolulu.
