§92-24 Directors of finance and commerce and consumer affairs; fees. Except as provided in section 91-2.5, the director of finance and the director of commerce and consumer affairs each shall charge the following fees: (1) For administering any oath, $1; (2) For preparing every photostat copy of any document on record in the director's office, 50 cents per page or portion thereof; (3) For preparing every typewritten copy of any document on record in the director's office, 50 cents per page or portion thereof; (4) For preparing a certificate of compliance, $5 for the original certificate, and $1 for each additional copy thereof, of which $4 from each certificate and 75 cents of each additional copy shall be deposited in the compliance resolution fund established pursuant to section 26-9(o); (5) For comparing any document submitted for certification, 15 cents per page or portion thereof; (6) For certifying any document on record in the director's office, 25 cents for each certification; (7) For all other acts and duties, the fees of which are not otherwise provided for, such charges as each may from time to time prescribe.
