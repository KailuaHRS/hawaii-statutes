[§356D-58] Lien attaches to personalty in possession. Sections 356D-56 and 356D-57 shall also apply to any personal property which, before May 2, 1949, was left in or about any public housing project, and was taken into the possession of the authority or its predecessor in interest, as herein set forth and provided. [L 2006, c 180, pt of §2] Note Section 356D-56 referred to in text is repealed. For present provision, see §356D-56.1. Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
