§8-30

Suicide Prevention and Awareness Month.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§8-30] Suicide Prevention and Awareness Month. The month of September shall be known and designated as "Suicide Prevention and Awareness Month". This month is not and shall not be construed as a state holiday. [L 2019, c 36, §2] Note Effective date changed from "July 1, 2050" to "July 1, 2024" by L 2024, c 5, §1.
