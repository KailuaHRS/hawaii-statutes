part XVII. TRANSITION PROVISIONS Note Part renumbered by L 2002, c 130, §37. §414D-321 Application to existing domestic corporations. This chapter applies to all domestic corporations in existence on July 1, 2002, that were incorporated under any general statute of this State providing for incorporation of nonprofit corporations.
