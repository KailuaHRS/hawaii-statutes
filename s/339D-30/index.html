§339D-30 Collector reporting requirements. By March 31, 2024, and annually thereafter, each collector shall report to the department the weight of all electronic devices collected for recycling and for reuse in the previous year. Reports shall be submitted on forms prescribed by the department and shall indicate the name of the recycler and weight of electronic devices sent to each recycler, and the name of the refurbisher and the number of and weight of electronic devices that were reused. Upon request, the department may require submission of bills of lading and recycler receiving reports for shipments to recyclers.
