§183-16 Revenue from forest reserves. Any moneys accrued from: (1) The harvest of non-native forest products from forest reserves; (2) The harvest of native forest products from degraded forests as defined in section 186-5.5, within forest reserves; (3) The sale of forest products found dead and lying on the ground; (4) The sale of tree seedlings from state nurseries; (5) The sale of any other products or services, or anything of value derived from forest reserves not described above; or (6) The imposition of fines or penalties for violations of this chapter and chapters 185 and 195F or any rule adopted thereunder, shall be deposited into the forest stewardship fund.
