§96-4 Procedure. The ombudsman may establish procedures for receiving and processing complaints, conducting investigations, and reporting the ombudsman's findings. However, the ombudsman may not levy fees for the submission or investigation of complaints. [L 1967, c 306, §5; HRS §96-4; gen ch 1985] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
