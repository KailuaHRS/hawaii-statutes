[§302A-1804] Student conference advisory committee. There is created a student conference advisory committee to assist and advise the student conference committee in planning, coordinating, and evaluating the annual conference of secondary school students. The department shall maintain guidelines and procedures for the establishment of the student conference advisory committee. [L 2013, c 156, pt of §1] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
