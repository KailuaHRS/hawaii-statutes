§167-3 Engineering program administrator. The board of agriculture and biosecurity shall appoint a registered professional engineer who shall act as engineering program administrator of the irrigation water development program and have such qualifications as the board may deem necessary. The appointment and removal of the engineering program administrator shall be in accordance with chapter 76 and the engineering program administrator shall perform the duties as set forth by the board.
