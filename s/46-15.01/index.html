[§46-15.01] Limitation of application. This chapter shall not be construed to exempt counties from the application of chapter 104 to experimental and demonstration housing projects pursuant to section 46-15. [L 1992, c 281, §1] Attorney General Opinions Chapter 104 applied to the county of Hawaii's Waikoloa employee housing project pursuant to this section and the plain language of §104-2. Att. Gen.
