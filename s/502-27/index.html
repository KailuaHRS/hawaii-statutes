§502-27 Charges. (a) Except when otherwise provided, fees for the use of microfilms of documents recorded in the bureau of conveyances for the purpose of making duplicates shall be established by rules adopted by the department of land and natural resources pursuant to chapter 91. (b) Frame charges for duplicating microfilm shall not be assessed against any agency of the State or counties thereof. [L 1974, c 7, §1; am L 1999, c 125, §4] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
