§326-27 Kalaupapa store; loans for operation and maintenance. (a) All moneys to enable the department of health to operate and maintain the Kalaupapa store, situated in Kalaupapa, Molokai, shall be allocated by the legislature through appropriations out of the state general fund. (b) The department shall include in its budgetary request for each upcoming fiscal period, the amounts necessary to effectuate the purposes of this section. (c) All moneys received in reimbursement of payments made under this section shall be deposited to the credit of the state general fund.
