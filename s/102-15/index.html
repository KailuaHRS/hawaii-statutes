§102-15 Violation voids contract. After May 31, 1959, any contract awarded or executed in violation of sections 102-1 to 102-12 shall be void. [L 1959, c 245, §11; Supp, §7B-11; HRS §102-15] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
