§232-2 Assessment in accordance with return no cause of grievance. No taxpayer shall be deemed aggrieved by an assessment to the extent that it is in accordance with the taxpayer's return. [L 1939, c 208, pt of §5; RL 1945, pt of §5211; RL 1955, §116-2; HRS §232-2; gen ch 1985] Cross References Not applicable to monthly returns, see §237-30. Case Notes Inapplicable where director of taxation changes assessment classification to one taxed at higher rate. 63 H. 579, 633 P.2d 535 (1981). Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
