§52D-13

Orders of retention.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§52D-13] Orders of retention. No property shall be sold or delivered under sections 52D-10 to 52D-14 if the chief of police, any county attorney or corporation counsel, or any court directs that it be retained as evidence. [L 1989, c 136, pt of §2] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
