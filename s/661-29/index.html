§661-29

Fees and costs of litigation.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§661-29] Fees and costs of litigation. The State shall not be liable for expenses or fees, including attorney fees, that a person incurs in bringing an action under this part and shall not elect to pay those expenses or fees. [L 2000, c 126, pt of §1] Previous Vol13_Ch0601-0676 Next
