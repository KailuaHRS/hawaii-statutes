[§340B-3.5] Wastewater treatment certification board special fund. (a) There is established in the state treasury a special fund to be known as the wastewater treatment certification board special fund. The following moneys collected by the board shall be deposited into the special fund: (1) Registration fees for examinations; (2) Renewal fees; (3) Reciprocity fees; and (4) Temporary certificates fees. All interest earned or accrued on moneys deposited into the special fund shall become a part of the special fund. (b) All moneys paid into the special fund shall be expended by the board to finance its operations. [L 1999, c 238, §1] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
