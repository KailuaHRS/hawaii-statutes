⚖ Official version ↗
[§256B-6] Account funds. Moneys in an ABLE savings account: (1) Shall be exempt from attachment, execution, or garnishment as provided by section 651-124; and (2) May be claimed by the medicaid plan subject to limitations imposed by the Secretary.
