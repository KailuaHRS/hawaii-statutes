§23G-11

Revisor of statutes.

⚖ Official version ↗
PART II. STATUTE REVISION AND PUBLICATION Note Uniform electronic legal material act, see chapter 98. §23G-11 Revisor of statutes. The director of the office of the legislative reference bureau, or a member of the staff of the bureau delegated by the director, shall be the revisor of statutes of the State.
