State mammal.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§5-12.5] State mammal. The Hawaiian monk seal (Monachus schauinslandi) is established and designated as the official mammal of the State. [L 2008, c 72, §2] Cross References Taking a monk seal prohibited, see §195D-4.5. Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
