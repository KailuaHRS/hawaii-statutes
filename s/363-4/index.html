§363-4 Establishment of cemeteries on Hawaii, Kauai, Maui, Molokai, and Lanai. The department of land and natural resources shall set aside, or acquire by exchange, purchase, or condemnation, in the manner provided by law, land suitable for the establishing of veterans cemeteries on the islands of Hawaii, Maui, Kauai, Molokai, and Lanai.
