§48E-19

Construction of this chapter.

⚖ Official version ↗
§48E-19 Construction of this chapter. The powers conferred by this chapter shall be supplemental to the powers conferred by any other law. Nothing contained in this chapter shall prohibit a county from providing for a pollution control project of the nature described in this part as a public undertaking, improvement, or system under chapter 47 or 49.
