[§342F-32] Complaints; hearings; appointment of masters. The director may: (1) Receive or initiate complaints of excessive noise, hold hearings in connection with excessive noise, and institute legal proceedings in the name of the State for the prevention, control, or abatement of excessive noise; and (2) Appoint a master or masters to conduct investigations and hearings.
