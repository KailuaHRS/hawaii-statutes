§281-114 Appeals. An appeal shall be allowed to any claimant of property or liquor seized as aforesaid from the judgment of the court in the same manner as appeals are allowed in other cases before such tribunals. If the claimant fails to appear and prosecute diligently the claimant's appeal, or fails to secure a reversal of the judgment in the appellate court, the judgment appealed from shall be carried out.
