[PART IVA.] EXOTIC ANIMALS; REIMBURSABLE INSPECTION [§159-31] Inspection of exotic animals. Persons desiring certification as to wholesomeness and unadulteration for meat and meat products derived from exotic animals may request the department to conduct an inspection under this chapter.
