§196D-13 Exemptions from certain state laws. In order to promote the purposes of this chapter, all persons hired by the department to effectuate this chapter are excepted from chapters 76 and 89. [L 1988, c 301, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 148, §18] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
