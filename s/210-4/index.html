§210-4 Functions, powers, and duties of director. In the performance of, and with respect to, the functions, powers, and duties vested in the director by this chapter, the director of business, economic development, and tourism may: (1) Prescribe rules and regulations to carry out this chapter. (2) Perform all functions necessary to effectuate the purposes of this chapter.
