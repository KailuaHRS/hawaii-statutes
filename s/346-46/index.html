§346-46 Certified nurse aides; training programs and recertification. The director shall: (1) Approve training programs for nurse aides and ensure that the training programs comply with all applicable federal and state requirements; (2) Approve and arrange for the recertification process for nurse aides who work in medicare or medicaid certified nursing facilities; (3) Approve and arrange for the recertification process for nurse aides who work in state-licensed or state-certified health care settings; and (4) Require recertification not less than every two years. The number of continuing education hours required for recertification shall not exceed twenty-four hours, except as specified by federal law.
