Duties of registered pharmacist.

⚖ Official version ↗
§461-11 Duties of registered pharmacist. Every registered pharmacist in charge of a pharmacy shall comply with all laws and rules. The pharmacist shall be responsible for the management of the pharmacy and every activity thereof which is subject to this chapter shall be under the pharmacist's complete control. All registered pharmacists shall notify the board of changes of business address within ten days.
