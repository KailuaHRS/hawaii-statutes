§322-34 Payment presumed when. All such liens shall be presumed to have been satisfied at the expiration of six years from the date of record. [L 1896, c 61, §10; am L 1915, c 149, §2; RL 1925, §988; RL 1935, §1338; RL 1945, §2729; RL 1955, §47-33; HRS §322-34] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
