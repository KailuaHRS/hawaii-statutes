Public schools special fees and charges.

⚖ Official version ↗
§302A-1130 Public schools special fees and charges. (a) The department may assess and collect special fees and charges from students for co-curricular activities. (b) Special fees and charges collected from students for co-curricular activities shall be deposited into insured checking or savings accounts and expended by each individual school.
