§88-94 Withholding of income taxes. Pension, annuity, and retirement allowance payments from the system shall be subject to income tax withholding requirements as set forth by the federal government; payment of these taxes as withheld shall be the liability of the system. The board of trustees shall adopt rules to administer the purposes of this section.
