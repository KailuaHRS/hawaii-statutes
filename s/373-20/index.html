Penalties.

⚖ Official version ↗
§373-20 Penalties. Any employment agency which violates this chapter is punishable upon conviction by a fine of not more than $1,000, or imprisonment for not more than six months, or both. [L 1959, c 264, pt of §3; Supp, §88A-20; HRS §373-20] Cross References Classification of offense and authorized punishment, see §§701-107, 706-640, and 706-663. Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
