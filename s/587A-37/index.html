PART V. MISCELLANEOUS [§587A-37] Failure to comply with terms and conditions of an order of the court. If a party fails to comply with the terms and conditions of an order issued by the court under this chapter, the court may apply the provisions of section 710-1077 and any other provisions available under the law.
