[PART VIII. HAWAII PERFORMANCE PARTNERSHIPS BOARD] [§27-51] Hawaii performance partnerships board; establishment. (a) There is established within the office of the governor, on a special and temporary basis, the Hawaii performance partnerships board. (b) The board shall consist of the following nine members who shall be appointed by the governor in accordance with section 26-34: (1) Three members representing business; (2) Three members representing philanthropy; and (3) Three members representing government. (c) Members shall serve for five-year terms, except for appointed state officials, who shall serve for the length of their appointments. (d) The members of the board shall serve without compensation and without reimbursement for expenses, including travel expenses. (e) The governor shall designate a chairperson from among the members of the board. [L 1999, c 160, §13] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
