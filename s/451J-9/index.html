§451J-9

Licensure fees.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§451J-9] Licensure fees. [Section effective until June 30, 2026. For section effective July 1, 2026, see below.] Licenses shall be valid for three years and shall be renewed triennially. Any applicant for renewal of a license that has expired within one year of the renewal deadline shall be required to pay a restoration fee in addition to all renewal fees. [L 1998, c 159, pt of §2] §451J-9 Licensure fees. [Section effective July 1, 2026. For section effective until June 30, 2026, see above.] Except as otherwise provided in section 451J-7.2, licenses shall be valid for three years and shall be renewed triennially. Any applicant for renewal of a license that has expired within one year of the renewal deadline shall be required to pay a restoration fee in addition to all renewal fees.
