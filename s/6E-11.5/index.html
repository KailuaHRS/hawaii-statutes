§6E-11.5 Civil penalties. Except as provided in section 6E-11, any person who violates this chapter, or any rule adopted pursuant to this chapter shall be fined not less than $500 nor more than $20,000 for each separate violation. Each day of each violation constitutes a separate violation. [L 2003, c 104, pt of §2; am L 2005, c 128, §4; am L 2022, c 212, §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
