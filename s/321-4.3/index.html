§321-4.3 Epidemiologists. The department of health may establish permanent or temporary exempt positions known as epidemiologists for the purpose of investigating diseases and injuries which threaten the public health and safety. The positions shall be appointed by the director without regard to chapter 76. [L 2008, c 78, §1; am L 2018, c 4, §2] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
