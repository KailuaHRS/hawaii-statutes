§328G-5 Laboratory standards and testing; certification. [See Note at beginning of chapter.] (a) The department shall establish and enforce standards for laboratory-based testing of crude extract and manufactured hemp products for content and contamination. (b) The department may certify laboratories and recognize certifications from other jurisdictions of laboratories that are qualified to test crude extract and manufactured hemp products for quality control prior to sale.
