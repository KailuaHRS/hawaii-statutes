Service of process.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§514B-106.5] Service of process. The board shall establish a policy to provide reasonable access to persons authorized to serve civil process in compliance with section 634-21.5. [L 2009, c 158, §§4, 8; am L 2011, c 65, §1] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
