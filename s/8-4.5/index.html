⚖ Official version ↗
[§8-4.5] Baha'i New Year's Day. The 21st day of March shall be known as Baha'i New Year's Day, provided that this day is not and shall not be construed to be a state holiday. [L 1971, c 39, §1] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
