§269E-1

Administration.]

⚖ Official version ↗
[§269E-1 Administration.] This chapter shall be administered by the public utilities commission and the provisions of this chapter and of chapter 269, not inconsistent with this chapter, shall govern its administration; provided that sections 269-7.5, 269-8.2, 269-8.5, 269-9, 269-16 to 269-28, 269-30, 269-31, 269-34 to 269-43, and 269-71 to 269-111 shall not, in any respect, apply to the one call center.
