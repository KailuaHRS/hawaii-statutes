§486-112 Declarations of unit price on random packages. In addition to the declarations required by section 486-111, any package that is one of a lot containing random measure of the same commodity and that bears the total selling price of the package, shall bear on the outside of the package a plain and conspicuous declaration of the price per single unit of measure, as established by rule of the board.
