[§489-23] Exclusion from civil rights commission. Notwithstanding the provisions of chapter 368, this part shall not be subject to chapter 368 and shall not be enforced by the civil rights commission. [L 2000, c 227, pt of §2] Previous Vol11_Ch0476-0490 Next
