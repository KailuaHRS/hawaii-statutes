[§355D-3] Enforcement. The courts, departments, agencies, and officers of the State or any county shall enforce this compact and shall do all things appropriate to effectuate its purposes and intent that may be within their respective jurisdictions, including but not limited to the making and submission of such reports as are required by the compact.
