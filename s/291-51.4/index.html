§291-51.4 Fraudulent verification of an applicant as a person with a disability; penalty. A physician, physician assistant, or advanced practice registered nurse who fraudulently verifies that an applicant is a person with a disability to enable the person to represent to the issuing agency that the person is qualified to obtain a disability parking permit shall be guilty of a petty misdemeanor. Each fraudulent verification shall constitute a separate offense. [L 2001, c 297, pt of §1; am L 2014, c 45, §6; am L 2019, c 87, §4; am L 2022, c 293, §3] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
