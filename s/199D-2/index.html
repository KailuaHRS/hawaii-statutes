[§199D-2] Applicability. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, all natural resource infractions that the department identifies as subject to administrative penalties may be adjudicated pursuant to this chapter. This chapter may be applied by all of the divisions of the department. [L 2004, c 142, pt of §2] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
