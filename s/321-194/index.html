[§321-194] State advisory commission. The state advisory commission on drug abuse and controlled substances established in section 329-2 shall advise the director on all matters relating to substance abuse including but not limited to the preparation of the state plan for substance abuse. In addition, the advisory commission shall perform such duties and assume such responsibilities as required by federal law for the purpose of receiving federal funding.
