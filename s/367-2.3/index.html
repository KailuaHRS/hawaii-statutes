Compensation.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§367-2.3] Compensation. The members shall serve without compensation but shall be reimbursed for their necessary expenses in attending meetings of the commission and in the discharge of their duties. [L 1991, c 46, §1] Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
