§651-46 Deeds, etc. The officer shall execute and deliver, to any purchaser at any such sale, such certificate of purchase, or conveyance, as may be necessary. [CC 1859, §1026; RL 1925, §2450; RL 1935, §4149; RL 1945, §10176; RL 1955, §233-45; HRS §651-46] Case Notes If invalid levy, no title passes to purchaser. 21 H. 745 (1913). Mortgage made by debtor after attachment being void, conveyance free of such mortgage did not convey a greater interest than the debtor had at time of sale. 51 H. 164, 454 P.2d 116 (1969). Cited: 16 H. 106, 110 (1904). Previous Vol13_Ch0601-0676 Next
