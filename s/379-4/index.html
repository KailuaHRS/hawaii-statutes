⚖ Official version ↗
§379-4 Penalty. Any person, or agent, or officer thereof, who violates this chapter is punishable by a fine of not more than $1,000 or imprisonment of not more than one year, or both. [L 1967, c 18, pt of §2; HRS §379-4] Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
