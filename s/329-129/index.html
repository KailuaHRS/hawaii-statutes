§329-129 Prohibited acts; flammable solvents. (a) No qualifying patient, primary caregiver, qualifying out-of-state patient, or caregiver of a qualifying out-of-state patient shall use butane to extract tetrahydrocannabinol from cannabis plants. (b) Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of a class C felony. [L 2015, c 241, pt of §5; am L 2018, c 116, §11] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
