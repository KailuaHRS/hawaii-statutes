§287-31 Reimbursement of insurer; proration of insurance. Any motor vehicle liability policy may provide that the insured shall reimburse the insurance carrier for any payment the insurance carrier would not have been obligated to make under the terms of the policy except for this chapter. Any motor vehicle liability policy may provide for the prorating of the insurance thereunder with other valid and collectible insurance.
