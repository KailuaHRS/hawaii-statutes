§347-7 Agencies for information and industrial aid. The department of human services shall maintain or cause to be maintained one or more agencies for employment information and industrial aid, the object of which shall be to aid the blind and visually handicapped persons in finding employment and shall provide instruction for such persons in trades and occupations which may be followed in their homes, and shall assist such persons in whatever manner it deems advisable in disposing of the products of their home industry.
