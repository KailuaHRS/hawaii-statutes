§507-19 Enforcement of lien. The holder of any lien provided for in section 507-18 may, upon obtaining judgment for the amount due for the repairs, alterations, or improvements, have execution upon the property so held by the holder for the satisfaction of the holder's judgment. The execution shall be issued and returned as in other civil actions.
