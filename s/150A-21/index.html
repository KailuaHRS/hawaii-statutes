Certification for shipment.

⚖ Official version ↗
PART III. NURSERY STOCK EXPORT SHIPMENTS §150A-21 Certification for shipment. The department may certify as to the pest condition or post treatment of shipments when officially required. Fees may be charged for certificates in certain instances and shall be deposited into the pest inspection, quarantine, and eradication fund.
