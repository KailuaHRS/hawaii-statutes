§652-15 Law applicable to all courts. This chapter and the powers conferred therein shall extend to all the common-law courts of the State, according to their jurisdiction. [L 1876, c 35, §13; RL 1925, §2844; RL 1935, §4283; RL 1945, §10315; RL 1955, §237-15; HRS §652-15] Case Notes "Common law courts" are those in contradistinction to courts of equity; district court has jurisdiction in proper cases. 19 H. 625 (1909).
