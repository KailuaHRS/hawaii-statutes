§271-37 Attorney general; aid in enforcement. Upon written request of the department, the attorney general shall prosecute all violations on behalf of the department for the enforcement of the provisions of this chapter. [L 1978, c 66, §6; am L 2024, c 117, §4] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
