PART II. POWER OF SALE FORECLOSURE PROCESS Note Part heading amended by L 2012, c 182, §3(7). Law Journals and Reviews Hawai‘i 2000 Report Regarding Lawyers' Opinion Letters in Mortgage Loan Transactions. 22 UH L. Rev. 347 (2000). §667-21 Power of sale process. The power of sale process in this part is an alternative to the foreclosure by action in part IA.
