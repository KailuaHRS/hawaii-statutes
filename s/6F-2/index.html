[§6F-2] Judiciary history center; establishment. There shall be established within the office of the administrative director of the courts, for administrative purposes only, the judiciary history center. The judiciary shall provide the center with facilities, security, janitorial services, utilities, fiscal, and necessary legal services. The center shall be located on the ground floor of the Ali‘iolani Hale Building. [L 1990, c 211, pt of §3] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
