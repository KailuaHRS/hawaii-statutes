[§342G-31 Guidelines.] The process of county and state integrated solid waste management planning shall be expedited to the greatest degree possible. The department, within six months of July 1, 1991, shall issue guidelines for the development of county plans. The draft guidelines shall be circulated to the counties for review and comment not less than forty-five days before their issuance. [L 1991, c 324, pt of §2] Revision Note "July 1, 1991" substituted for "the effective date of this chapter". Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
