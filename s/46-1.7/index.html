[§46-1.7] Retention of emergency 911 recordings. Each county public safety answering point shall retain recordings of all emergency 911 telephone calls and radio dispatches for a period of not less than one year. [L 2005, c 192, §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
