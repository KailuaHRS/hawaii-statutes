[§8-25] Human Trafficking Awareness Month. The month of January shall be known and designated as "Human Trafficking Awareness Month" to promote public awareness of human trafficking as a significant societal and public health crisis. This month is not and shall not be construed to be a state holiday. [L 2013, c 246, §1] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
