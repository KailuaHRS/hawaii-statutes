[§461J-10.13] Authorized providers of continuing competence units. Continuing competence units shall be obtained from a provider or agency approved by the board, including but not limited to: (1) Continuing education courses, including home- and self-study courses, obtained from an agency recognized by the board; (2) College coursework from an educational institution accredited by the United States Department of Education or other agency recognized by the board; and (3) Other competence related activities approved by the board or an agency recognized by the board.
