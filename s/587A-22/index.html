[§587A-22] Unavailability of specified privileges. The following privileges shall not be available to exclude evidence of imminent harm, harm, or threatened harm in any proceeding under this chapter: (1) The physician-patient privilege; (2) The psychologist-client privilege; (3) The spousal privilege; and (4) The victim-counselor privilege.
