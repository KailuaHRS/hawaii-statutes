[§454M-2.5] Unlicensed foreclosure actions voided. Any action taken in connection with a mortgage foreclosure under chapter 667 by a nonexempt person who engages in the business of mortgage servicing without a license as provided in this chapter shall be void for purposes of chapter 667.
