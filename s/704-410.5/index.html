[§704-410.5] Conditional release; duration limited in nonfelony cases. For any defendant granted conditional release in a nonfelony case pursuant to section 704-411(1)(b), 704-412, 704-414, or 704-415, the period of conditional release shall not exceed one year. [L 2016, c 231, pt of §3] COMMENTARY ON §704-410.5 Act 231, Session Laws 2016, added this section to implement recommendations made by the Penal Code Review Committee convened pursuant to House Concurrent Resolution No. 155, S.D. 1 (2015).
