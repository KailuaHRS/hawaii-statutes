PART III. SOURCE SELECTION AND CONTRACT FORMATION §103D-301 Methods of source selection. Unless otherwise authorized by law, all contracts shall be awarded pursuant to the following sections, as applicable: (1) Section 103D-302 (Competitive sealed bids); (2) Section 103D-303 (Competitive sealed proposals); (3) Section 103D-304 (Professional services procurement); (4) Section 103D-305 (Small purchases); (5) Section 103D-306 (Sole source procurement); and (6) Section 103D-307 (Emergency procurements).
