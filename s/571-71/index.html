PART VII. JUVENILE CRIME PREVENTION BUREAU §571-71 Juvenile crime prevention bureau; establishment of. Any chief of police may establish as a subdivision of the police department under the chief's jurisdiction a juvenile crime prevention bureau, to be maintained and conducted as hereinafter provided.
