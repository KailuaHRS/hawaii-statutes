§291C-106

Speed limit for Daniel K. Inouye highway.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§291C-106] Speed limit for Daniel K. Inouye highway. Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, the speed limit for the Daniel K. Inouye highway shall be sixty miles per hour beginning at mile marker 12; provided that the speed limit shall be forty-five miles per hour or any other speed limit as determined pursuant to sections 291C-102, 291C-104, or 291C-105, beginning at the Pohakuloa training area and ending one-half mile east of the Mauna Kea county park entrance.
