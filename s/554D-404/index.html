§554D-404

Trust purposes.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§554D-404] Trust purposes. A trust may be created only to the extent its purposes are lawful, not contrary to public policy, and possible to achieve. A trust and its terms shall be for the benefit of its beneficiaries, subject to the provisions of the trust. [L 2021, c 32, pt of §2] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
