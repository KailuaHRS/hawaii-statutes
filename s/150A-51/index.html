[PART VI.] BIOSECURITY PROGRAM Note Invasive species management requirements, including interisland and export database for agricultural commodities. L 2016, c 243, §3. §150A-51 Biosecurity program; establishment. The department shall establish a biosecurity program authorized under this chapter that shall interface with other relevant state law; provided that the biosecurity program is not inconsistent with federal law. The department shall act as the lead agency for the State's biosecurity efforts. [L 2008, c 236, pt of §2; am L 2024, c 231, §3] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
