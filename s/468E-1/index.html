§468E-1 Purpose. It is the policy and the purpose of this chapter to insure that only qualified persons be allowed to practice in the fields of speech pathology and audiology. [L 1974, c 48, pt of §1; am L 1988, c 320, §2] Previous Vol10_Ch0436-0474 Next
