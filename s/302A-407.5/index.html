[§302A-407.5] School bus fare revolving fund. (a) There is established in the state treasury the school bus fare revolving fund, into which shall be deposited all moneys received from students and parents or guardians of students by public schools for state-provided school busing services, as authorized by section 302A-406. (b) Except as otherwise provided by the legislature, moneys in the school bus fare revolving fund shall be used for school busing services, as authorized by section 302A-406.
