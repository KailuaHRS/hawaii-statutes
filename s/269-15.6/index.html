§269-15.6

Alternative dispute resolution.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§269-15.6] Alternative dispute resolution. The commission may require the parties in any matter before the commission to participate in nonbinding arbitration, mediation, or other alternative dispute resolution process prior to the hearing. [L 2000, c 263, §1] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
