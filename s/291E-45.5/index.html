[§291E-45.5] Repeat intoxicated driver after December 31, 2010; eligibility to obtain motor vehicle registration and number plates. Any repeat intoxicated driver arrested for a violation of section 291E-61 or 291E-61.5 after December 31, 2010, may request that the director remove any stopper imposed on the motor vehicle registration files pursuant to part III of chapter 291E. Upon request, the director shall remove the stopper as soon as practicable. [L 2012, c 327, §3] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
