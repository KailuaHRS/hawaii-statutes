§353L-5 Access to records by oversight coordinator and commission. The department of corrections and rehabilitation shall provide full access to all information requested by the oversight coordinator and commission. [L 2019, c 179, pt of §2; am L 2022, c 278, §29] Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
