[§667-36.5] Bundled properties; prohibition. Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, for the public sale of mortgaged property under the power of sale foreclosure process in this part, no mortgagee shall bundle mortgaged properties for the purpose of public sale and each mortgaged property shall be bid on separately, unless the deed or mortgage otherwise requires.
