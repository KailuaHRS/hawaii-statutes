⚖ Official version ↗
§431P-2 Establishment of Hawaii hurricane relief fund. There shall be a Hawaii hurricane relief fund to be placed within the department of commerce and consumer affairs for administrative purposes. The fund shall be a public body and a body corporate and politic. Any applicant for insurance from the fund shall provide proof, to the satisfaction of the board, of the inability to obtain hurricane property insurance from insurers licensed to transact business in the State.
