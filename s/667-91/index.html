[PART VI.] ASSOCIATION ALTERNATE POWER OF SALE FORECLOSURE PROCESS [§667-91] Alternate power of sale process. The power of sale process in this part is an alternative process for associations to the foreclosure by action in part IA and the foreclosure by power of sale in part II.
