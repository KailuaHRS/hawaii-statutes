[PART VIII.] HEEIA COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT DISTRICT §206E-201 Definitions. As used in this part: "District" means the Heeia community development district. "Fund" means the Heeia community development special fund. [L 2011, c 210, pt of §2; am L Sp 2021, c 9, §28] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
