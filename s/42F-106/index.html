§42F-106 Monitoring and evaluation. Every grant shall be monitored by the expending agency to ensure compliance with this chapter and the public purpose and legislative intent of the grant. [L 1997, c 190, pt of §3; am L 2014, c 96, §10] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
