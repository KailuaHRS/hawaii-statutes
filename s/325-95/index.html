[§325-95] Prevention plan; screening; treatment support. The department shall develop a statewide, comprehensive hepatitis prevention plan. The plan shall include hepatitis screening and services to support individuals with hepatitis to access care and treatment. The department may work with other government agencies, health care providers, health insurers, and community stakeholders to improve overall rates of screening, diagnosis, and treatment of hepatitis.
