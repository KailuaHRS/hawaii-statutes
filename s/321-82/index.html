§321-82 Field nutritionist, rural Oahu. The director of health may appoint a field nutritionist for rural Oahu and establish, or arrange for the establishing of, a suitable office therefor. [L 1951, c 305, §1; RL 1955, §46-23; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §321-82] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
