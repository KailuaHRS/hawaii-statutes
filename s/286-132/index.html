§286-132 Driving while license suspended or revoked. Except as provided in section 291E-62, no resident or nonresident whose driver's license, right, or privilege to operate a motor vehicle in this State has been canceled, suspended, or revoked may drive any motor vehicle upon the highways of this State while the license, right, or privilege remains canceled, suspended, or revoked. [L 1937, c 234, §29; RL 1945, §7330; RL 1955, §160-62; HRS §286-132; am L 1970, c 164, §3; am L 1982, c 251, §4; am L 1985, c 258, §3; am L 1996, c 169, §2; am L 2001, c 157, §7] Case Notes Driving without license under §286-102 not lesser included offense of driving while license suspended under this section. 81 H. 76 (App.), 912 P.2d 573 (1996).
