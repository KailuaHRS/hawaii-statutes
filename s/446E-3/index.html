§446E-3 Sanction. Any person who violates this chapter shall be deemed to have engaged in an unfair or deceptive act or practice pursuant to section 480-2. [L 1979, c 135, pt of §2; am L 1999, c 171, §4] Case Notes Defendants' argument that this section shifts the burden of proof from the plaintiffs to the defendants and that the burden shifting is unconstitutional, lacked merit. 486 F.
