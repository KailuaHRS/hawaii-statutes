§554D-811

Enforcement and defense of claims.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§554D-811] Enforcement and defense of claims. (a) A trustee shall take reasonable steps to enforce claims of the trust and to defend claims against the trust. (b) A trustee may abandon or assign to one or more of the beneficiaries of the trust any claim that it believes is not prudent to enforce. [L 2021, c 32, pt of §2] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
