[§514B-64] Private consultants. The director of commerce and consumer affairs may contract with private consultants for the review of documents and information submitted to the commission pursuant to this chapter. The cost of the review by private consultants shall be borne by the developer. [L 2005, c 93, pt of §3] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
