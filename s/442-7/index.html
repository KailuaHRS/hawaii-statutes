§442-7 Application, scheduling, administration, scoring, and place of examination. The application requirements and candidate qualifications to take the National Board of Chiropractic Examiners examinations specified in section 442-6, and the scheduling, administration, reexamination, scoring, and place of the examinations required for licensure shall be in accordance with the policies and requirements of the National Board of Chiropractic Examiners, unless otherwise determined and modified by the board. The qualifications for licensure in this State shall be as established in this chapter and chapter 436B and in rules adopted by the board.
