[§468M-11] Violations; fraud. Any violations by an activity desk of any law relating to client trust accounts or the bond or letter of credit pursuant to section 468M-10 shall constitute a prima facie showing of fraud on the part of the activity desk and may not be dischargeable as a debt to a consumer or an activity provider in any bankruptcy proceeding.
