[§327-25] Uniformity of application and construction. In applying and construing this uniform act, consideration shall be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to its subject matter among states that enact it. [L 2008, c 122, pt of §1] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
