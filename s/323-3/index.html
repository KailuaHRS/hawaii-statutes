§323-3 Practice by advanced practice registered nurses. Each hospital in the State licensed under section 321-14.5 shall allow advanced practice registered nurses licensed pursuant to section 457-8.5 and qualified advanced practice registered nurses granted prescriptive authority pursuant to section 457-8.6 to practice at the hospital within the full scope of practice authorized under chapter 457, including practice as a primary care provider.
