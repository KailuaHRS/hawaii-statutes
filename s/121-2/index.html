§121-2 Exemptions. The following are exempt from military duty: all persons exempted from military duty under the laws of the United States, all judges of the several courts, and the members of the legislature. [L 1967, c 196, pt of §1; HRS §121-2] Revision Note Reference to "magistrates" following "judges", deleted as unnecessary in view of L 1970, c 188. Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
