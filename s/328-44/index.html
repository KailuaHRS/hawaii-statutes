§328-44 Bad food; bait. No storer or operator shall place in cold storage any article of food if diseased, tainted, or deteriorated so as to injure its keeping qualities or if not properly slaughtered, handled, and prepared for storage; provided that this section shall not apply to bait to be used for fishing purposes. All such bait shall be stored in rooms specially set aside for such purposes. [L 1925, c 194, §4; RL 1935, §1066; RL 1945, §2235; RL 1955, §51-43; HRS §328-44] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
