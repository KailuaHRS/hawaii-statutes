[§124B-180] Endangerment offenses. (a) Any person subject to this chapter who engages in conduct that: (1) Is wrongful and reckless or is wanton; and (2) Is likely to produce death or grievous bodily harm to another person, shall be punished as directed by a court-martial. (b) Any person subject to this chapter who: (1) Fights or promotes a fight, or is concerned in or connives a fight; or (2) Having knowledge of a challenge to fight sent or about to be sent, fails to report the facts promptly to the proper authority, shall be punished as directed by a court-martial. (c) Any person subject to this chapter who, wilfully and wrongly, discharges a firearm under circumstances as to endanger human life shall be punished as directed by a court-martial. (d) Any person subject to this chapter who unlawfully carries a dangerous weapon concealed on or about their person shall be punished as directed by a court-martial.
