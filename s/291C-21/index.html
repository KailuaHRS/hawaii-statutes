RULES OF THE ROAD PART III. OBEDIENCE TO AND EFFECT OF TRAFFIC LAWS [§291C-21] Provisions of parts III to XIV refer to vehicles upon the highways; exceptions. The provisions of parts III to XIV relating to the operation of vehicles refer exclusively to the operation of vehicles upon highways except, where a different place is specifically referred to in a given section.
