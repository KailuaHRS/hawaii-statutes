§171-36.2 Public lands for historic preservation and restoration. (a) Any law to the contrary notwithstanding, the board may lease public lands in the State for use in historic preservation and restoration projects: (1) Through negotiations; and (2) For a price which shall be determined by the board. (b) The department shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 to determine what constitutes historic preservation and restoration projects for the purposes of this section; provided that no definition or criteria established shall conflict with any federal, state, or county law. (c) All subleases of land disposed of pursuant to this section shall be subject to the approval of the board. [L 1985, c 33, §2; am L 2002, c 114, §1] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
