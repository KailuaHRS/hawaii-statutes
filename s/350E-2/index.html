[§350E-2] Financial responsibility for child placed under compact. Financial responsibility for any child placed pursuant to the provisions of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of Article V of the compact in the first instance. However, in the event of partial or complete default of performance thereunder, the provisions under any other chapter also may be invoked. [L 1985, c 132, pt of §1] Previous Vol07_Ch0346-0398 Next
