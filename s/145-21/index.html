PART II. OWNERSHIP AND MOVEMENT OF AGRICULTURAL COMMODITIES §145-21 Definitions. For purposes of this part: "Agricultural commodity" means any fruit, nut, or vegetable that is the food product of any tree, vine, or plant, or any aquacultural, horticultural, silvicultural, or floricultural product. "Person" means any individual, firm, corporation, partnership, or association. [L 1999, c 186, pt of §2; am L 2003, c 20, §1] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
