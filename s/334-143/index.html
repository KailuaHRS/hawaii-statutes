§334-143

Notice of hearing and service of petition on respondent.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§334-143] Notice of hearing and service of petition on respondent. The court shall set a time and date for hearing on the petition, within ten days of the filing of the petition. The notice of hearing and petition shall be served on the respondent. [L 2004, c 44, pt of §20] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
