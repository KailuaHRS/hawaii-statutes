§206E-122 Loans; insurance and guarantees. The authority may procure insurance or guarantees against any default of its loans, in amounts and from insurers or guarantors, as it deems necessary or desirable. [L 1982, c 228, pt of §8] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
