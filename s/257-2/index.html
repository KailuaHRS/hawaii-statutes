§257-2

Eligible individuals.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§257-2] Eligible individuals. (a) The income of the household of the individual development account holder shall not exceed eighty per cent of the area household median income. (b) The individual shall enter into an individual development account agreement with a fiduciary organization. [L 1999, c 160, pt of §25] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
