[§606-16] Study for annual adjustments of fees for interpreters. Beginning in 2026 and every three years thereafter, the judiciary shall conduct a study of interpreter fees, including recommendations on adjustments to the fee rates, and submit its findings and recommendations to the chief justice for consideration.
