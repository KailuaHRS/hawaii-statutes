⚖ Official version ↗
§143-14 Female dogs. It shall be unlawful for the owner of any female dog, licensed or unlicensed, to permit it to run at large while the dog is in the copulating season. [L 1941, c 268, §12; RL 1945, §7163; RL 1955, §156-14; HRS §143-14] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
