C. Revocation of Certificate of Authority §414-461 Grounds for revocation. The department director may commence a proceeding under section 414-462 to revoke the certificate of authority of a foreign corporation authorized to transact business in this State if: (1) The corporation fails to: (A) Pay any fees prescribed by law; (B) File its annual report for a period of two years; (C) Appoint and maintain an agent for service of process as required; or (D) File a statement of a change in the name or business address of the agent as required; or (2) A misrepresentation has been made of any material matter in any application, report, affidavit, or other record or document submitted by the corporation.
