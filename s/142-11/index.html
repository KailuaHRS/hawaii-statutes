§142-11

Forfeiture of animals, etc.

⚖ Official version ↗
§142-11 Forfeiture of animals, etc. All animals, fodder, fittings, or effects landed contrary to this chapter or taken or removed from quarantine before being duly discharged, shall be forfeited to the use of the State. All animals brought into quarantine grounds or placed with any animals under quarantine or placed into provisional quarantine, shall be deemed to come under this chapter and shall be subject to all the conditions of the same.
