§40-93 Form of notice to appear for examination. To............................... By virtue of the authority vested in me by chapter 40 of the Hawaii Revised Statutes, you are hereby required to appear before me at........................on........................and to bring with you for the purpose of examination a certain book (or as the case may be) and to be examined by and before me touching and concerning matters required to be investigated by me pursuant to the provisions of the chapter. Witness my hand this ..... day of ....., 19..... .............................. Comptroller.
