§141-53 Fees for aquaculture services. The department of agriculture and biosecurity may establish and assess fees pursuant to chapter 91 for: (1) Aquatic animal and plant health diagnostic services; and (2) Any items or expert services purchased from the department related to aquaculture planning, aquaculture disease management, and the marketing of seafood products; provided that the assessment of these fees does not violate any provisions of this chapter.
