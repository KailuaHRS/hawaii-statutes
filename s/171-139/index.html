[§171-139] Acquisition. The board may acquire by exchange, direct purchase, or eminent domain, lands to which private persons or other public agencies hold title for designation as or inclusion in an industrial park. The acquisition of lands shall be in accordance with this chapter. [L 1988, c 361, pt of §1] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
