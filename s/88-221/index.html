§88-221 Payments by political subdivisions. Each political subdivision whose plan has been approved under section 88-218 shall pay to the contribution fund, with respect to wages (as defined in section 88-211), at such time or times as the state agency may by regulation prescribe, contributions in the amounts and at the rates specified in the applicable agreement entered into by the state agency under section 88-212.
