[§201H-87] Applications for financing; application periods. If sufficient funding is available, the corporation shall open at minimum two application periods each year for the receipt of applications for financing from parties interested in applying for financing for the development of affordable housing in the State from the low-income housing tax credit program, Hula Mae multi-family program, rental housing revolving fund program, and dwelling unit revolving fund program. Applications for the four programs may be consolidated for administrative efficiency. [L 2023, c 91, §1] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
