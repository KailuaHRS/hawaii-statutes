⚖ Official version ↗
§330-9 Penalty. Any person who violates section 330-8 shall, upon conviction, be punished by a fine not exceeding $100 or by imprisonment not exceeding three months, or by both. [L 1963, c 115, pt of §2; Supp, §53-5.6; HRS §330-9] Cross References Classification of offense and authorized punishment, see §§701-107, 706-640, and 706-663. Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
