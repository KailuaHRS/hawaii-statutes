§53-14 Agency exempt from real property taxes. The redevelopment agency shall be exempt from real property taxes upon any of its land or buildings so long as title is held by it, but not exceeding two years from the date of their acquisition; provided that the tax exemption shall not apply to any land or structure from which the agency is receiving an income.
