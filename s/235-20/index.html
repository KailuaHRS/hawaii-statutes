⚖ Official version ↗
[§235-20] Comfort letters; authority to assess fees; established. The department may assess and collect a fee for the issuance of any comfort letter of the department. All fees collected under this section shall be deposited into the tax administration special fund established under section 235-20.5. [L 2004, c 215, pt of §3] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
