§342J-10.5

Disposition of collected fines and penalties.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§342J-10.5] Disposition of collected fines and penalties. Fines and penalties collected under sections 342J-9 and 342J-10 shall be deposited into the environmental response revolving fund established by section 128D-2. [L 1991, c 157, §5] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
