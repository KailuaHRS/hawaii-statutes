§11-426 Candidate exceeds voluntary expenditure limit. A candidate who files the affidavit agreeing to limit expenditures and who exceeds the expenditure limit for that election shall: (1) Notify all opponents, the office of elections, and the commission by telephone and writing on the day the expenditure limit is exceeded; (2) Pay the balance of the full filing fee; and (3) Provide reasonable notice to all contributors within thirty days of exceeding the limit that the expenditure limit was exceeded.
