[§8-23] Homelessness Awareness Month. The month of November shall be known and designated as "Homelessness Awareness Month" to promote public awareness of homelessness as a significant societal, public health and welfare, and public housing shortage problem. This month is not and shall not be construed as a state holiday. [L 2013, c 50, §1] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
