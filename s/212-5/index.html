§212-5 Setting aside public lands; lease, license, and permits. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, the governor may set aside public lands to a public corporation for the purpose of this chapter and the corporation may, by negotiation and without recourse to public auction, lease, rent, or let the lands or any part thereof or any interest therein, including concessions and concession space, by lease, license, or permit on the terms and conditions set by the corporation.
