[§200-18] Records of suspensions and revocations of operating privileges to be maintained. (a) The department shall maintain a record of all persons adjudicated of violations under part III of chapter 291E and the period of suspension or revocation of operator privileges ordered by the director under that part. (b) The department shall maintain a record of all persons convicted of offenses or violations under part IV of chapter 291E and the period of suspension or revocation of operator privileges ordered by the court under that part.
