PART II. PLANNING COORDINATION AND IMPLEMENTATION Note Part heading reenacted. L 1991, c 76, part of §1. §226-51 Purpose. The purpose of this part is to establish a statewide planning system to coordinate and guide all major state and county activities and to implement the overall theme, goals, objectives, policies, and priority guidelines.
