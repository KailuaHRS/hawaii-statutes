§501-266

Dual recording involving deregistered land.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§501-266] Dual recording involving deregistered land. Nothing in this part shall prevent or prohibit the registration of an instrument that conveys, assigns, or affects both registered land and deregistered land. [L 2009, c 120, pt of §2, §21; am L 2013, c 119, §11] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
