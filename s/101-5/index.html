§101-5 Fee simple or lesser estate may be acquired. In the taking of private property for a public use, a fee simple estate or any lesser estate may be acquired. [L 1896, c 45, §2; RL 1925, §810; RL 1935, §52; RL 1945, §303; am L 1951, c 12, §1(d); RL 1955, §8-5; HRS §101-5] Case Notes Condemning a fee simple estate subject to existing tenancy. 42 H. 415. Undivided interests in parcels of land owned in fee simple are fee simple estates and may be taken in eminent domain proceedings. 50 H. 501, 443 P.2d 140. Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
