§666-13 Effect of writ. Whenever a writ is issued for the removal of any tenant, the contract for the use of the premises, if any exists, and the relation of landlord and tenant between the parties, shall be deemed to be canceled and annulled. [CC 1859, §945; RL 1925, §2786; RL 1935, §4023; RL 1945, §10412; RL 1955, §240-13; HRS §666-13] Case Notes Warrant terminates relation of landlord and tenant only upon final judgment for possession. 27 H. 362, 368 (1923).
