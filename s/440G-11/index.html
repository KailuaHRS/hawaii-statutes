§440G-11 Rate, filed with director; approval. (a) The director shall require each cable operator to file a schedule of its rates of service on a form and with the notice that the director may prescribe. (b) To the extent permitted by federal law, the director shall regulate rates to ensure that they are fair both to the public and to the cable operator.
