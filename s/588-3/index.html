§588-3

Director, program administrative staff.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§588-3] Director, program administrative staff. The program shall be headed by a director appointed by the administrative director of the courts. The director and administrative staff shall be subject to chapter 76. [L 1986, c 169, pt of §1; am L 2000, c 253, §150] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
