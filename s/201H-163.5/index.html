[§201H-163.5] Downpayment loan assistance program; fees. The corporation may establish, revise, charge, and collect fees, premiums, and charges as necessary, reasonable, or convenient, for its downpayment loan assistance program. The fees, premiums, and charges shall be deposited into the housing finance revolving fund established in section 201H-80. [L 2017, c 123, §2] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
