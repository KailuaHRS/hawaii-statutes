§386-53 Nonweekly periodic payments. On the application of either party, the director may, having due regard for the welfare of the employee or the employee's dependents and the convenience of the employee's employer, authorize benefits to be paid fortnightly, semimonthly, monthly or quarterly instead of weekly.
