§37C-12

Debt affordability study.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§37C-12] Debt affordability study. The director of finance shall submit to the legislature a debt affordability study prior to the convening of the regular session of each odd-numbered year. The director shall submit the study with the multi-year program and financial plan. [L 2015, c 149, pt of §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
