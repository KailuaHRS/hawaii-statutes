§448-9.4 American Board of Dental Examiners (ADEX) examination; regional examinations. An applicant shall take and pass the ADEX examination, and neither the state examination nor any regional examination shall be accepted. [L 2005, c 121, §3; am L 2007, c 72, §1] Previous Vol10_Ch0436-0474 Next
