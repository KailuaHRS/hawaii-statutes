§342F-11.5

Disposition of collected fines and penalties.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§342F-11.5] Disposition of collected fines and penalties. Fines and penalties collected under sections 342F-9 and 342F-11 shall be deposited into the environmental response revolving fund established by section 128D-2. [L 1991, c 157, §3] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
