⚖ Official version ↗
[§8-42] Laulau Day. The first Friday in May of each year shall be known and designated as "Laulau Day". This day is not and shall not be construed to be a state holiday. [L 2025, c 270, §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
