PART IV. HAWAII YOUTH CONSERVATION CORPS Note Part heading amended by L 2015, c 43, §2. [§193-31] Authorization. The governor is authorized to avail the State of the benefits of any law or laws of the United States, now existing or to be enacted, such as Title 16 United States Code, sections 1701 to 1706, or any other law or laws of similar purport.
