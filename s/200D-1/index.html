§200D-1

Definitions.

⚖ Official version ↗
§200D-1 Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context clearly requires otherwise: "Council" means the Kaneohe Bay regional council. "Lead agency" means the department of land and natural resources. "Master plan" means the Kaneohe Bay master plan developed pursuant to Act 208, Session Laws of Hawaii 1990, and amended by section 200D-3. "Members" means the members of the Kaneohe Bay regional council.
