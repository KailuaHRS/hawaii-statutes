§88F-4

Compensation and expenses.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§88F-4] Compensation and expenses. Each trustee shall serve without compensation but shall be reimbursed by the plan for any necessary expense incurred in the performance of the trustee's duties pertaining to the plan. [L 1996, c 212, pt of §2] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
