[§340B-7] Regulations. The board shall adopt such rules and regulations as may be necessary for the administration of this chapter, and which shall include at least the following provisions: (1) The basis for classification of wastewater treatment plants as required by section 340B-2; (2) Criteria for the qualification of applicants for operator certification corresponding to each of the classifications referred to in paragraph (1) of this section; (3) Procedures for examination of candidates and renewal of certificates; and (4) Procedures for the revocation of certificates.
