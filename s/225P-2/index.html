Definitions.

⚖ Official version ↗
§225P-2 Definitions. [See Note at beginning of chapter.] As used in this chapter: "Commission" means the Hawaii climate change mitigation and adaptation commission. [L 2014, c 83, pt of §2; am L 2017, c 32, §4] Note Repeal of chapter on July 1, 2022, by L 2017, c 32, §§6, 10, as shown in the main volume, deleted by L 2018, c 15, §4(2). The source note to this section is amended to read as follows: "L 2014, c 83, pt of §2; am L 2017, c 32, §§4, 6, and 10; am L 2018, c 15, §4". Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
