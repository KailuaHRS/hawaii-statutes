[§657D-53] Revocation of interlocutory orders. Any interlocutory order under this chapter, made upon the court's own motion, may be revoked, modified, or extended by it upon appropriate notice to the parties. [L 1994, c 257, pt of §2] Previous Vol13_Ch0601-0676 Next
