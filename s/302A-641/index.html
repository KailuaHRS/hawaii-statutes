[§302A-641] Meal count assistants, adult supervisors, and classroom cleaners; minimum wage. Except as provided in section 387-9, the wages of meal count assistants, adult supervisors, and classroom cleaners of the department shall be no less than the current state minimum wage as required in section 387-2.
