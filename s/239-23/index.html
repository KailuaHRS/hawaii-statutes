[§239-23] Mobile telecommunications definitions. The definitions relating to mobile telecommunications services set forth under section 239-22 shall apply to give effect to the federal Mobile Telecommunications Sourcing Act, title 4 United States Code sections 116 to 126, and shall have no impact on the interpretation of the laws of this State except as expressly set forth in this chapter.
