§88E-4 Composition of the board of trustees. The board of trustees shall consist of seven members as follows: (1) The director of human resources development of the State or a designated representative, ex officio; (2) The director of finance of the State or a designated representative, ex officio; and (3) Five other persons, who shall be public employees and represent employee interests.
