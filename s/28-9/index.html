§28-9 Additional deputies for acquisition of rights-of-way. The attorney general may appoint and employ deputy attorneys general, additional to those covered by the appropriation for the department of the attorney general, to provide legal services for the acquisition of rights-of-way and for proceedings therefor, and the compensation for such services shall be paid by the state department of transportation from the state highway fund.
