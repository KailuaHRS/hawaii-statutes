§421I-2

Amendment of bylaws.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§421I-2] Amendment of bylaws. The bylaws of a corporation may be amended only upon receiving the affirmative vote or written consent of at least two-thirds of the shareholders. [L 1993, c 282, pt of §1] Previous Vol08_Ch0401-0429 Next
