[§571-22.5] Appeal of waiver of jurisdiction. An order waiving jurisdiction shall not be appealable as a final order, but may only be appealable in conjunction with an appeal of all other issues after a trial on the charge against such minor or adult. [L 1980, c 207, §1] Case Notes Does not permit appellate review of order denying waiver of jurisdiction. 66 H. 516, 668 P.2d 25 (1983). Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
