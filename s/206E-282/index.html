[§206E-282] Ninety-nine year leasehold program. (a) There is established the ninety-nine year leasehold program for the purpose of providing low-cost, leasehold residential condominium units for sale to Hawaii residents on state-owned lands within an urban redevelopment site. (b) The program shall be limited to one project on non-ceded lands within an urban redevelopment site, which shall be selected by the authority.
