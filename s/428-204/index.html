§428-204 Articles of amendment. A limited liability company may amend its articles of organization from time to time, in any and in as many respects as may be desired, so long as its articles of organization as amended contain only those provisions which may be lawfully contained in original articles of organization at the time of making the amendment. The articles of amendment shall be delivered to the director for filing and shall set forth: (1) The name of the limited liability company; and (2) The amendment to the articles of organization, referencing specifically the provisions being amended.
