§235-23 Taxable in another state. For purposes of allocation and apportionment of income under this part, a taxpayer is taxable in another state if: (1) In that state the taxpayer is subject to a net income tax, a franchise tax measured by net income, a franchise tax for the privilege of doing business, or a corporate stock tax, or (2) That state has jurisdiction to subject the taxpayer to a net income tax regardless of whether, in fact, the state does or does not.
