§255D-3 Authority to participate in multistate negotiations. For the purposes of reviewing or amending the agreement embodying the simplification requirements in section 255D-6, the State may enter into multistate discussions. For purposes of these discussions, the State shall be represented by the department. [L 2003, c 173, pt of §2; am L Sp 2005, c 3, §3] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
