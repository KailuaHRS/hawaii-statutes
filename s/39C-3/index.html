[§39C-3] Application of allocation. Any issuer that issues bonds to which an allocation of the state ceiling has been applied, shall evidence and report the application by a certificate, a copy of which shall be sent to the department. The department shall maintain a record of all allocations of the state ceiling made by the department and copies of certificates sent to or retained by the department.
