[§321-34] Cytomegalovirus public education. (a) The department of health shall provide public education to inform pregnant women and women who may become pregnant regarding: (1) The transmission of the cytomegalovirus to pregnant women and women who may become pregnant; (2) Birth defects caused by congenital cytomegalovirus; (3) Methods of diagnosing congenital cytomegalovirus; and (4) Available preventative measures. (b) The department of health shall provide the information described in subsection (a) to: (1) Hospitals licensed pursuant to section 321-14.5; (2) Health care providers offering care to pregnant women and infants; (3) Registered family child care homes; (4) Licensed group child care centers; (5) Licensed group child care homes; (6) Licensed infant and toddler child care centers; and (7) Religious, ecclesiastical, or denominational organizations offering children's programs as a part of worship services.
