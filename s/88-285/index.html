§88-285 Service-connected disability retirement. A member who would be eligible to receive a service-connected disability retirement allowance pursuant to section 88-79 shall receive a maximum retirement allowance of thirty-five per cent of the member's average final compensation. [L 1984, c 108, pt of §8; am L 2001, c 101, §5; am L 2004, c 179, §28; am L 2005, c 58, §20] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
