[§136-1] Definitions. As used in this chapter, "laser pointing device" means any hand-held laser device, which is not designed as a sighting device for a weapon nor for use in a medical procedure. This term includes the commercially-available device, which is commonly known as a "laser pen" or "laser pointer". [L 1999, c 94, pt of §1] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
