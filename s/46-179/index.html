§46-179

Fees and costs of litigation.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§46-179] Fees and costs of litigation. A county shall not be liable for expenses or fees, including attorney fees, that a person incurs in bringing an action under this part and shall not elect to pay those expenses or fees. [L 2001, c 227, pt of §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
