[§844D-74] Not a basis for invalidation of identification or suppression of identification evidence. Nothing in this part shall be construed to invalidate an otherwise valid identification of a person by means of DNA testing or to provide the basis for a suppression of otherwise admissible evidence related to identification of a person based on DNA testing.
