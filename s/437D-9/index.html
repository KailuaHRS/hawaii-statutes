§437D-9 Posting requirements. Except as provided in section 437D-17, each lessor who offers the damage waiver shall conspicuously display at the rental area of each rental location a notice, in plain language and printing, that includes all of the information in section 437D-5(a)(1), (2), (5), and (6), and a statement that restrictions or conditions apply.
