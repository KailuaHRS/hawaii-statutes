Division functions.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§489D-33] Division functions. (a) The division shall exercise all administrative functions of the State in relation to the regulation, supervision, and licensing of money transmitters. (b) The division shall interpret and carry out the provisions of this chapter. [L 2006, c 153, pt of §1] Previous Vol11_Ch0476-0490 Next
