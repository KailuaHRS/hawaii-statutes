§11-383

Exceptions.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§11-383] Exceptions. Notwithstanding sections 11-381 and 11-382: (1) A party may support more than one candidate; and (2) A candidate for the office of governor or lieutenant governor may support a co-candidate in the general election. [L 2010, c 211, pt of §2] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
