§445-120 Enforcement by civil suit. Injunction forbidding the erection, maintenance, or display of, or commanding the removal or alteration of any outdoor advertisement or billboard, which does not comply with this statute or any ordinance enacted under the authority of this statute shall be granted upon the suit of the State by the attorney general, or of the county in which the outdoor advertisement or billboard is or probably will be erected, maintained, or displayed, or of the owner of any land, building, or part of a building from which such outdoor advertisement is or will be visible and so conspicuous as to draw attention. Any owner of a freehold or leasehold in such property shall be deemed such owner and entitled to bring such suit.
