§379A-2

Penalty.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§379A-2] Penalty. Any person violating section 379A-1 shall be fined not more than $200 or imprisoned not more than six months, or both. [L Sp 1949, c 8, §3; RL 1955, §300-3; HRS §757-2; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1] Cross References Classification of offense and authorized punishment, see §§701-107, 706-640, and 706-663.
