§353D-3 Offender family service center program; established. (a) There is established in the department an offender family service center program near the Oahu community correctional center. (b) The department may contract with a private nonprofit agency pursuant to chapter 42D to implement this chapter. [L 1987, c 251, pt of §1; am L 1991, c 335, §13] Note The amendment made by L 1997, c 190, §6 is not included in this section. Chapter 42D referred to in text is repealed.
