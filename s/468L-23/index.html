[§468L-23] Charter tour client trust account. Every travel agency engaged in the business of a charter tour operator shall establish and maintain a separate charter tour client trust account solely for the purpose of the travel agency's charter tour business. The charter tour client trust account shall be maintained in accordance with sections 468L-5 and 468L-24. [L 1999, c 258, pt of §3] Previous Vol10_Ch0436-0474 Next
