§440-2

Commission established.

⚖ Official version ↗
§440-2 Commission established. There shall be a board which shall be known as the state boxing commission of Hawaii. The commission shall consist of five members. At least one of the members shall be a member of USA Boxing, Inc. One member shall be designated by the governor as chairperson of the commission.
