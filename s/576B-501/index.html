§576B-501 Employer's receipt of income withholding order of another state.

⚖ Official version ↗
ARTICLE 5. ENFORCEMENT OF SUPPORT ORDER WITHOUT REGISTRATION Note Article heading amended by L 2015, c 77, pt of §1. §576B-501 Employer's receipt of income withholding order of another state. An income withholding order issued in another state may be sent by or on behalf of the obligee, or by the support enforcement agency, to the person defined as the obligor's employer under sections 571-52, 571-52.2, 571‑52.3, 576D-14, and 576E-16, without first filing a petition or comparable pleading or registering the order with a tribunal of this State.
