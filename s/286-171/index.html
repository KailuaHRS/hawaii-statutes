§286-171

Statewide traffic records system.

⚖ Official version ↗
PART VIII. TRAFFIC RECORDS §286-171 Statewide traffic records system. (a) There is established a statewide traffic records system. The state director of transportation shall be responsible for the administration and operation of the system and for this purpose shall adopt necessary rules and regulations pursuant to chapter 91. (b) The statewide traffic records system shall include all traffic records of the violation bureaus of the district courts, the circuit courts, the police departments, the county [directors of finance], the department of health, and the department of education and all dispositions pertaining to administrative license revocation proceedings conducted by the administrative director of the courts.
