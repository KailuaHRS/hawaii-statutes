§90-5 Agency reports, required information. A state agency as part of its annual report to the governor, the legislature, or the chief justice shall include estimates of: (1) The total number of volunteers and the total number of hours of service broken down into categories of regular-service volunteers, occasional volunteers, stipended volunteers, and material donors. (2) A list of volunteer job titles used by the agency. [L 1978, c 10, pt of §2; am L 1998, c 295, §3] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
