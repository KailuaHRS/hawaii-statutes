[§517E-9] Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act. This chapter shall modify, limit, and supersede the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. section 7001 et seq., but shall not modify, limit, or supersede section 101(c) of that Act, 15 U.S.C. section 7001(c), or authorize electronic delivery of any of the notices described in section 103(b) of that Act, 15 U.S.C. section 7003(b).
