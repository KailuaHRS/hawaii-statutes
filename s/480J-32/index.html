[§480J-32] Exemptions; relation to other laws. (a) This chapter shall not apply to the following: (1) A financial institution; (2) A nondepository financial services loan company; (3) An "open end credit plan", as defined in the Truth in Lending Act, title 15 United States Code section 1602(j); or (4) A tax refund anticipation loan. (b) Persons licensed under this chapter shall not be subject to chapter 443B. [L 2021, c 56, pt of §2] Previous Vol11_Ch0476-0490 Next
