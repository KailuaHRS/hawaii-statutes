PART XVIII. TRANSITION PROVISIONS [§414-481] Application to existing domestic corporations. This chapter applies to all domestic corporations in existence on July 1, 2001 that were incorporated under any general statute of this State providing for incorporation of corporations for profit if the power to amend or repeal the statute under which the corporation was incorporated was reserved. [L 2000, c 244, pt of §1] Revision Note "July 1, 2001" substituted for "its effective date". Previous Vol08_Ch0401-0429 Next
