§289-8 Penalties. (a) Whoever violates any of the provisions of this chapter or any lawful rule or regulation promulgated by the director of finance under authority of this chapter, for the violation of which no penalty is provided by law, shall be fined not less than $25 nor more than $500. (b) Any person who violates section 289-2, 289-9, or 289-10 shall be guilty of a class C felony. [L 1967, c 277, §9; HRS §289-8; am L 2022, c 88, §5] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
