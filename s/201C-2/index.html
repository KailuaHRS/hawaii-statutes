[§201C-2] Establishment. The department of business, economic development, and tourism shall establish a financial services assistance program for the purpose of promoting the growth and development of a financial services industry and other support services including the telecommunications infrastructure. The department may also hire contractors and consultants to effectuate the purpose of this chapter. [L 1990, c 107, pt of §3] Revision Note "Chapter" substituted for "Act" pursuant to §23G-15.
