§173-3 Authorization of revenue bonds. The department of land and natural resources may issue, as provided by part III of chapter 39, revenue bonds in a total amount not exceeding $25,000,000 to finance in whole or in part the costs of construction of new land areas under this [chapter], and other costs as provided in section 173-1.
