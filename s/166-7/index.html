§166-7 Applicants. Any person, including a revocable living trust, partnership, corporation, limited liability company, association, or an agricultural cooperative organized under chapter 421 at least seventy-five per cent of the trustees, partners, officers and stockholders, or members of which qualify individually, may apply for an agricultural park lease if the person, trustees, partners, officers and stockholders, or members are eligible and qualified according to criteria set forth in rules adopted by the board in conformance with section 166-9.
