§195D-10 Severability. Should any section, subsection, sentence, clause, or phrase of this chapter, or any rule adopted pursuant thereto be for any reason held by a court of competent jurisdiction to be invalid, such decision shall not affect the validity of the remaining portions of this chapter or rule adopted pursuant thereto. [L 1975, c 65, pt of §1; am L 1983, c 111, §11] Note The amendment made by L 2014, c 218, §8 is not included in this section. Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
