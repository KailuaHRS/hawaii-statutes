§291C-225 Failure to comply with summons or citation. If a violator of this part does not return an answer in response to a summons or citation within a period of fourteen days upon receipt of the summons or citation, the traffic, emergency period, and fireworks violations bureau shall issue to the registered owner of the vehicle a penal summons ordering the registered owner's appearance in court.
