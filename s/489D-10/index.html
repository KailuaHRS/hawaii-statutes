§489D-10 Application and license fees. (a) Each application shall be accompanied by: (1) A nonrefundable application fee in the amount of $5,000; and (2) An initial annual license fee of $5,000. (b) The license fee shall be refunded if the application is denied. [L 2006, c 153, pt of §1; am L 2008, c 195, §6; am L 2013, c 167, §6] Previous Vol11_Ch0476-0490 Next
