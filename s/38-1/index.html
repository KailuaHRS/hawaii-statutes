§38-1 Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicate: Comptroller means the state comptroller. Depository includes any federally insured national or state bank, savings and loan association, or financial services loan company; or federal or state credit union insured by the national credit union administration authorized to do business in this State. Director means the state director of finance. [L 1970, c 51, pt of §1; am L 1988, c 27, §1 and c 78, §2; am L 1989, c 266, §3] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
