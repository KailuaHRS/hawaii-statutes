[§188-22.7] Miloli`i fisheries management area. The Miloli`i fisheries management area, as defined in the administrative rules of the department of land and natural resources, shall be designated a community-based subsistence fishing area, as provided in section 188-22.6. The department of land and natural resources shall adopt management strategies and other rules consistent with section 188-22.6 that: (1) Ensure long-term sustainable populations of fish and other marine species; and (2) Encourage the scientific study and understanding of subsistence fishing management.
