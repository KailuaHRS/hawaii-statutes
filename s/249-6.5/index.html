[§249-6.5] Exemption for National Guard, military reserves, and other active duty military personnel claiming Hawaii as their residence of record. One noncommercial motor vehicle registered to a member of the National Guard, military reserve, or armed service, including the Coast Guard, who is: (1) Assigned to a unit in the State; and (2) A member in good standing, shall be exempt from the vehicle weight tax provided for in this chapter.
