§174C-32 Coordination. (a) Respective portions of the water resource protection and water quality plans, and the water use and development plans of each county, shall be developed together to achieve maximum coordination. (b) The development of the Hawaii water plan or any portion thereof shall proceed in coordination with and with attention to the Hawaii state plan described in chapter 226. (c) The Hawaii water plan and its constituent parts, except for the water quality plan, shall be adopted by the commission not later than three years from July 1, 1987. The commission shall receive the water quality plan from the department of health and incorporate this part in the Hawaii water plan. [L 1987, c 45, pt of §2; am L 1999, c 197, §3] Revision Note "July 1, 1987" substituted for "the effective date of this chapter". Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
