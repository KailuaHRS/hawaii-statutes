§708-826 Criminal tampering in the first degree. (1) A person commits the offense of criminal tampering in the first degree if, and with intent to cause a substantial interruption or impairment of a service rendered to the public by a utility or by an institution providing health or safety protection, the person damages or tampers with, without the consent of the utility or institution, its property or facilities and thereby causes substantial interruption or impairment of service. (2) Criminal tampering in the first degree is a misdemeanor. [L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1993] Previous Vol14_Ch0701-0853 Next
