⚖ Official version ↗
[§328-12.4] Food establishments; use of latex gloves prohibited. The use of latex gloves by personnel in a food establishment permitted under title 11, chapter 50 of the Hawaii administrative rules, shall be prohibited. [L 2016, c 180, §4] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
