[§46-165] Other rights not affected. Nothing in this part shall be construed to invalidate any provision relating to the transference or purchase of development rights heretofore or hereafter adopted by any county. [L 1998, c 296, pt of §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
