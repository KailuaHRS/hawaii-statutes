§187A-4 Administrator. The board shall appoint an administrator of aquatic resources who shall have charge, direction, and control of all matters relating to aquatic resources management, conservation, and development activities under this title, and such other matters as the board may direct. The administrator shall be trained and educated in natural resource management.
