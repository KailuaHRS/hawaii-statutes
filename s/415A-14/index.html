§415A-14 Directors and officers. Not less than one-half of the directors of a professional corporation and all of the officers, other than the secretary and the treasurer, shall be qualified persons with respect to the corporation. At least one director shall be a resident of this State. [L 1985, c 259, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 135, §119] Previous Vol08_Ch0401-0429 Next
