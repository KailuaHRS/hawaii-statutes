[§141D-12] Public lands exemption. Notwithstanding chapter 171, disposition of lands set aside for use pursuant to this chapter shall not be subject to the prior approval of the board of land and natural resources. [L 2022, c 221, pt of §1] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
