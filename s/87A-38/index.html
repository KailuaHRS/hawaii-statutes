[§87A-38] State and county contributions not considered wages or salary. Contributions made by the State or the counties under this part shall not be considered wages or salary of an employee-beneficiary. No employee-beneficiary shall have any vested right in or be entitled to receive any part of any contribution made to the fund. [L 2001, c 88, pt of §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
