§87A-8

Compensation and expenses.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§87A-8] Compensation and expenses. Each trustee shall serve without compensation, but the trustees may be reimbursed from the fund for any reasonable expenses incurred in carrying out the purposes of the fund. [L 2001, c 88, pt of §1] Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
