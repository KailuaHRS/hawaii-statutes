§128D-2.6 Ecological risk assessor. The department may establish a permanent exempt position for an ecological risk assessor for the purpose of assessing ecological risks and damages. The position shall be appointed by the director without regard to chapter 76. [L 1999, c 211, §2; am L 2000, c 253, §150; am L 2015, c 185, §8] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
