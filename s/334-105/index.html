§334-105

Evaluation.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§334-105] Evaluation. An adequate proportion of funds shall be applied to the development and implementation of an appropriate evaluation mechanism. The department, in conjunction with the programs, shall specify the criteria to be used in the evaluation of each program and in the evaluation of the entire system. By June 30, 1982, the department shall conduct or contract for the evaluation. Evaluation reports shall be transmitted to the legislature. [L 1980, c 221, pt of §2] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
