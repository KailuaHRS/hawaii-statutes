[§342P-25] False statements. (a) Any person who knowingly makes a false material statement, representation, or certification in any application, record, report, plan, or other document filed or required to be maintained under this chapter or who knowingly falsifies, tampers with, or renders inaccurate any monitoring device or method required to be maintained under this chapter, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor. (b) A person that is an organization, upon conviction of violating this section, shall be fined not more than $20,000. [L 1991, c 219, pt of §1] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
