[§304A-1006] Compensation of education laboratory school cafeteria personnel. The compensation of all cafeteria workers and the cafeteria manager at the education laboratory school cafeteria, or any successor organization or unit, shall be paid out of the general fund of the State. The university shall include, in its budgetary request for each upcoming fiscal period, the amounts necessary to carry out the purposes of this section.
