§441-10 Proration and consolidation of existing improvement district encumbrances in order to clear such encumbrances prior to dedication. Notwithstanding section 441-4 exempting dedicated cemetery property from subdivision laws, the map or plat required by section 441-3 may be treated for purposes of proration or consolidation of any improvement district lien as a subdivision or consolidation thereof.
