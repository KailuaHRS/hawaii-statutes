§88D-1

Definitions.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§88D-1] Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise: "Employee" shall have the same meaning as defined in section 76-11. "Jurisdiction" shall have the same meaning as defined in section 76-11. "Special pay plan" means a governmental retirement plan that meets the requirements of sections 401(a) and 414(d) of the Internal Revenue Code, and which may be a qualified pick-up plan under section 414(h)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code.
