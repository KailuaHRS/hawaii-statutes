⚖ Official version ↗
[§200-62] Trespass to vessel; penalty. Whoever, without right, boards or remains in or upon any vessel of another within the waters of the State shall be guilty of a misdemeanor. [L 1991, c 272, pt of §2] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
