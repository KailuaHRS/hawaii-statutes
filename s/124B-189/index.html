[§124B-189] Misprision of serious offense. Any person subject to this chapter: (1) Who knows that another person has committed a serious offense; and (2) Wrongfully conceals the commission of the offense and fails to make the commission of the offense known to civilian or military authorities as soon as possible, shall be punished as directed by a court-martial.
