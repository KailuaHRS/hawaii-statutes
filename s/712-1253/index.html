§712-1253 Penalties under other laws. Any penalty imposed for violation of this chapter or chapter 329 is in addition to, and not in lieu of, any civil or administrative penalty or sanction otherwise authorized by law. [L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 176, §9] Previous Vol14_Ch0701-0853 Next
