[§174C-82] Powers and duties of the commission. In addition to its other powers and duties, the commission shall: (1) Require registration of all existing wells, as provided in section 174C-83; (2) Require permits for well construction and for installation of pumps and pumping equipment as provided in section 174C-84; (3) Require well completion reports, as provided in section 174C-85; (4) Develop well construction and installation standards for pumps and pumping equipment, as provided in section 174C-86; and (5) Adopt, modify, and enforce all rules and orders necessary to carry out this part.
