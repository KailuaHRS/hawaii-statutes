[§104-2.7] Provisions of law; waiver. No provision of this chapter may in any way be contravened or set aside by private contract. [L 2014, c 130, §1] Note Section applies to all contracts entered into on or after July 1, 2014. L 2014, c 130, §9. Previous Vol02_Ch0046-0115 Next
