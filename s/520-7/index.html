Rights.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§520-7] Rights. No person shall gain any rights to any land by prescription or otherwise, as a result of any usage thereof for recreational purposes as provided in this chapter. [L 1969, c 186, §7] Case Notes Circuit court correctly determined that the State obtained an easement over and across seawall pursuant to the common law doctrine of implied dedication because this section and §§26-7, 107-10, 171-30, and 264-1(c)(1) do not "imperatively require" abrogation of common law implied dedication, nor do they evince an express legislative intent to do so. 140 H. 437, 403 P.3d 214 (2017).
