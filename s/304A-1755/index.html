§304A-1755 Residency program; defined. For the purpose of this subpart, "residency program" means a graduate medical education program in a hospital in this State that is accredited as a medical school residency program by the school of medicine of the University of Hawaii. The school of medicine may develop a special residency program for the purpose of this subpart; provided that the program, upon completion by the person, qualifies the person to take the licensure examination under chapter 453.
