[§206N-4] Zoning. Small wireless facilities and associated modified or replaced utility poles subject to the height limits in section 206N-5(c), shall be classified as permitted uses and shall not be subject to zoning review or zoning approval if they are deployed: (1) In the right of way in any zone; or (2) Outside the right of way in property not zoned exclusively for conservation. Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to modify existing permitting processes for the placement of wireline backhaul in the right of way.
