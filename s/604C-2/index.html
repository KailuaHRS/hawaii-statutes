§604C-2

Community outreach court; establishment.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§604C-2] Community outreach court; establishment. (a) The community outreach court shall be established as a division of the district court of the first circuit. (b) The community outreach court shall be held at any duly designated location within the first judicial circuit by any designated judge of the community outreach court.
