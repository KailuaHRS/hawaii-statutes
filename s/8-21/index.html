[§8-21] Domestic Violence Awareness Month. The month of October shall be known and designated as "Domestic Violence Awareness Month" to promote public awareness of domestic violence as a significant societal, public health, and criminal justice problem. This month is not and shall not be construed as a state holiday. [L 2012, c 73, §1] Previous Vol01_Ch0001-0042F Next
