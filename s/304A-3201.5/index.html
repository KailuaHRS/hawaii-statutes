Definitions.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§304A-3201.5] Definitions. As used in this subpart, unless the context clearly requires otherwise: "Commission" means the Western Interstate Commission for Higher Education. "Hawaii commission" means the Hawaii western interstate commission for higher education established under section 304A-3201.6. "Hawaii commissioners" means the members of the Hawaii western interstate commission for higher education. [L 2012, c 137, pt of §1] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
