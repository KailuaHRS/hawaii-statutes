§291C-4 Safe routes to school program special fund; establishment. There shall be established in the state treasury the safe routes to school program special fund, into which shall be deposited: (1) Assessments collected for speeding in a school zone, pursuant to section 291C-104; (2) Safe routes to school program surcharges collected in accordance with sections 291-16 and 291C-5; (3) Moneys collected from state reserved parking space enforcement fines pursuant to sections 291-57 and 291-72; [(4)] The surcharge collected pursuant to section 249-37; and [(5)] Fines collected for street parking restrictions pursuant to section 291C-116.
