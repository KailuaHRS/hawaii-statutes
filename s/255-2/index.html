[§255-2 Representation on multistate commission.] The director of taxation, or the director's designee, shall represent the State on the Multistate Tax Commission. The attorney general, or the attorney general's designee, shall act as an alternate for the director of taxation. [L 1968, c 30, §3; gen ch 1993] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
