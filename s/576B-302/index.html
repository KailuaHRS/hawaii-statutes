§576B-302 Proceeding by minor parent. A minor parent, or a guardian or other legal representative of a minor parent, may maintain a proceeding on behalf of or for the benefit of the minor's child. [L 1997, c 295, pt of §1; am L 2015, c 77, pt of §1] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
