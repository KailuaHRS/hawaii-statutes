[§482C-4] Lawful uses; defined. This chapter shall not apply to any person who transfers or causes to be transferred any sounds intended for or in connection with radio or television broadcast transmission or related uses, for archival purposes, or solely for the personal use of the person transferring or causing the transfer and with no purpose of capitalizing commercially on such reproduction.
