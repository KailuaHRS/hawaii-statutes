[§124B-164] Public record offenses. Any person subject to this chapter who, wilfully and unlawfully: (1) Alters, conceals, removes, mutilates, obliterates, or destroys a public record; or (2) Takes a public record with the intent to alter, conceal, remove, mutilate, obliterate, or destroy the public record, shall be punished as directed by a court-martial.
