[§27-54] Data collection relating to the well-being of Hawaii's children and families. (a) The Hawaii performance partnerships board with the assistance of the center on the family at the University of Hawaii at Manoa, shall establish key indicators and data sets relevant to the health, education, and socioeconomic well-being of Hawaii's children and families. (b) The Hawaii performance partnerships board may request and shall receive from every department, division, board, bureau, commission, or other agency of the State and its political subdivisions, cooperation and assistance in the performance of its duties relating to results and performance accountability, decision-making, and budgeting. [L 2001, c 274, §1] Revision Note Section was enacted as an addition to chapter 304 but is renumbered to this chapter pursuant to §23G-15. Cross References Departmental data sharing, see §27-7.
