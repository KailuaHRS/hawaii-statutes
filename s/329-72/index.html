[§329-72] Rules. [Section effective until December 31, 2023. For section effective January 1, 2024, see below.] The department of public safety may adopt rules and assess reasonable fees relating to the registration and control of the sale, distribution, or possession of regulated chemicals under part VI of chapter 329. [L 2004, c 193, pt of §1] §329-72 Rules. [Section effective January 1, 2024. For section effective until December 31, 2023, see above.] The department of law enforcement may adopt rules and assess reasonable fees relating to the registration and control of the sale, distribution, or possession of regulated chemicals under part VI of chapter 329.
