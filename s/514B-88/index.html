§514B-88 Delivery. In this part, delivery shall be made by: (1) Personal delivery; (2) Registered or certified mail with adequate postage to the recipient's address; provided that delivery shall be considered made three days after deposit in the mail or on any earlier date upon which the return receipt is signed; (3) Facsimile transmission, if the recipient has provided a fax number to the sender; provided that delivery shall be considered made upon the sender's receipt of automatic confirmation of transmission; (4) Electronic mail; or (5) Any other way prescribed by the commission.
