§662-6 Pleadings, trial and appeal. [(a)] The Hawaii rules of civil procedure and the district court rules of civil procedure as applicable shall be followed in any action under this chapter. A certified copy of all pleadings shall be duly served on the attorney general. (b) Section 661-2 shall apply to actions under this chapter. [L 1957, c 312, pt of §1; Supp, §245A-6; HRS §662-6; am L 1972, c 164, §2(b); am L 1978, c 156, §3; am L 2017, c 12, §70] Note Section 661-9 referred to in text is repealed.
