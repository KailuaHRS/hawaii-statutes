[§334B-4] Waiver. This chapter shall not apply to a review agent that operates under contract with the federal government for utilization review activities relating to recipients and health care providers under Title XVIII of the Social Security Act, Title XIX of the Social Security Act, and the Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Uniformed Services (CHAMPUS).
