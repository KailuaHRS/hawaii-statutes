[§658J-17] Correction by arbitrator of unconfirmed award. Upon motion of a party made no later than thirty days after an arbitrator gives notice under section 658J-15(a) of an award, the arbitrator may correct the award: (1) If the award has an evident mathematical miscalculation or an evident mistake in the description of a person, thing, or property; (2) If the award is imperfect in a matter of form not affecting the merits on the issues submitted; or (3) To clarify the award.
