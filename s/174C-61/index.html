§174C-61

Fees.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§174C-61] Fees. The commission shall promulgate a schedule of application and permit fees. The fees shall be used to defray the administrative costs of the permit systems established under this chapter. A public agency shall not be subject to the payment of any fees.
