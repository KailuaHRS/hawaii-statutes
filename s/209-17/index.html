§209-17 Use of funds in relation to federal projects. The funds allocated to this part shall be expended by the Hawaii housing finance and development corporation only upon the finding that the housing project found necessary does not qualify for federal aid or participation. [L 1961, c 189, §11; Supp, §98P-11; HRS §209-17; am L 1986, c 339, §14; am L 1987, c 337, §9(2); am L 1997, c 350, §14; am L 2005, c 196, §26(b); am L 2006, c 180, §16] Cross References Public lands for disaster relief, see §171‑93.
