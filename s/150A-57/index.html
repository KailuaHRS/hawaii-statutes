§150A-57 Annual report. The department shall submit an annual report on the biosecurity program to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session of the legislature. The report shall include: (1) The schedule required under section 150A-56; (2) The status of each activity required by Act 243, Session Laws of Hawaii 2016, including for each activity: (A) All expenditures; (B) Descriptions of and the purposes of any activity-related travel; (C) Workforce allocation; and (D) Measure of effectiveness; (3) Summary of interisland inspections and export inspection activities; (4) Projections by year of future expenditures and future acreage to be under pest management by crop; (5) Activities yet to be completed, and, if applicable, an explanation why they were not completed; and (6) Real-time geographic information system map data, coordinated data collection, work plans, memorandums of understanding, and contracts for service related to advancing the State's invasive pest control and eradication biosecurity efforts.
