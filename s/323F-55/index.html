[§323F-55] Transfer or assignment of other business assets. Provisions to transfer ownership or assign the interest of the corporation or the Maui regional system in some or all of the equipment and furnishings of the facility or facilities transferred to the private entity or its nonprofit management entity under this part shall be included in the lease entered into under section 323F-54.
