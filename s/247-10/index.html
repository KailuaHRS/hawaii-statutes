§247-10 Collection. All revenue laws relating to the assessment and collection of taxes are hereby extended to and made a part of this chapter, so far as applicable, for the purpose of collecting the tax imposed herein. [L 1966, c 10, pt of §1; HRS §247-10] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
