Office of public guardian; establishment; appointment.

⚖ Official version ↗
[§551A-1] Office of public guardian; establishment; appointment. There is established the office of the public guardian in the judiciary. The chief justice shall appoint the public guardian, who shall serve at the chief justice's pleasure. [L 1984, c 223, pt of §1] Previous Vol12_Ch0501-0588 Next
