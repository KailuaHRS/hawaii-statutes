§386-122 Notice of insurance. If the insurance so effected is under section 386-121(a)(1), the employer shall file with the director in a form prescribed by the director a notice of the employer's insurance together with a statement of benefits provided by the policy of insurance. The director may also accept the notice of employer's insurance from approved third party agencies in a manner and form approved by the director. [L 1963, c 116, pt of §1; Supp, §97-121; HRS §386-122; gen ch 1985; am L 1986, c 304, §4; am L 1988, c 35, §1; am L 2012, c 262, §1] Case Notes Cited: 27 H. 476, 480 (1923); 32 H. 162 (1931).
