PART VIII. STREAM DIVERSION WORKS [§174C-91] Definition. In this part: "Stream diversion works" means any artificial or natural structure emplaced within the stream for the purpose of diverting stream water. [L 1987, c 45, pt of §2] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
