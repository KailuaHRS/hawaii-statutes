[§211D-10] Reports; audits. (a) The department shall submit to the legislature an annual status report on the program's activities. (b) The financial transactions of the fund are subject to audit by the auditor. [L 2000, c 290, pt of §2] Previous Vol04_Ch0201-0257 Next
