§271-38 Enforcement. The department shall assign a motor vehicle safety officer employed by the department to assist in the enforcement of this chapter. [L 1995, c 105, §§3, 7; am L 1996, c 102, §1; am L 1997, c 120, §6; am L 2004, c 199, §2; am L 2024, c 117, §5] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
