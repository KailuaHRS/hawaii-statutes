[PART XXXIX.] SERVICES TO ADULTS Cross References Inspections of state-licensed care facilities; public notice, see §321-1.8. Law Journals and Reviews Long-Term Care in Hawaii. 18 HBJ, no. 13, at 59 (2015). [§321-491] Purpose. The purpose of this part is to establish the nature and type of services to elders, disabled, and aged who are qualified to receive social services according to standards and conditions prescribed by the department of health.
