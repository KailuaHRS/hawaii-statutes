§130-5 Applicability. This chapter shall control and be supreme in the event it shall be employed notwithstanding any statutory, charter, or ordinance provision to the contrary or in conflict herewith. [L 1959, c 16, §5; Supp, §359A-5; HRS §130-5] Previous Vol03_Ch0121-0200D Next
