[§658J-24] Appeal. (a) An appeal may be taken under this chapter from: (1) An order denying a motion to compel arbitration; (2) An order granting a motion to stay arbitration; (3) An order confirming or denying confirmation of an award; (4) An order correcting an award; (5) An order vacating an award without directing a rehearing; or (6) A final judgment. (b) An appeal under this section may be taken as from an order or a judgment in a civil action. [L 2017, c 113, pt of §1] Previous Vol13_Ch0601-0676 Next
