§323D-14 Functions; statewide health coordinating council. The statewide council shall: (1) Prepare and revise as necessary the state health services and facilities plan; (2) Advise the state agency on actions under section 323D-12; (3) Appoint the review panel pursuant to section 323D-42; and (4) Review and comment upon the following actions by the state agency before such actions are made final: (A) The making of findings as to applications for certificate of need; and (B) The making of findings as to the appropriateness of those institutional and noninstitutional health services offered in the State.
