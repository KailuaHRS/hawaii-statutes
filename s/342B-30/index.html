[§342B-30] Judicial review. The applicant and any person who participated in the public comment process may obtain judicial review in [environmental court] of the final action on a permit issuance or renewal. This is in addition to judicial review otherwise available. [L 1992, c 240, pt of §1; am L 2014, c 218, §8] Previous Vol06_Ch0321-0344 Next
