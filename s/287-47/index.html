§287-47 Other relief available. Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as preventing the plaintiff in any action from obtaining relief upon process provided by any provision of law. [L 1949, c 393, pt of §1; RL 1955, §160-125; HRS §287-47] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
