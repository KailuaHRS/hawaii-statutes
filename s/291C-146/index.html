⚖ Official version ↗
§291C-146 Carrying articles. No person operating a bicycle shall carry any package, bundle, or article which prevents the use of both hands in the control and operation of the bicycle. A person operating a bicycle shall keep at least one hand on the handlebars at all times. [L 1971, c 150, pt of §1; am L 1984, c 273, §13] Previous Vol05_Ch0261-0319 Next
