Chapter 255

MULTISTATE TAX COMPACT]

⚖ View this chapter on the official State site ↗
  • §255-1Adoption of compact.
  • §255-2Representation on multistate commission.] The director of taxation, or the director's designee, shall represent the State on the Multistate Tax Commission.
  • §255-3Adoption of interstate audits.] Article VIII of the Multistate Compact relating to interstate audits shall be in force in and with respect to this State.
